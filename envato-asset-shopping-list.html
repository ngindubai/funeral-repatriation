Current Gap

The site now has the Virtuary structure and color language, but visual coverage is still thin for sections that should rotate fresh photography (hero, services, testimonials, and informational pages). We kept the existing hero and grief-focused images as requested, then identified missing assets that will make the design feel complete and intentional.

Critical point: The site should avoid generic hospital stock photos. Favor dignified, respectful funeral logistics and family-support visuals.
