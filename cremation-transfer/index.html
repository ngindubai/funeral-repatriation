Cremation abroad and ashes transport to the UK

For families considering cremation in the country where their loved one died, and carrying the ashes home personally. These country guides cover the process, documentation, and airline rules.

Get Help Now

Before you decide: speak to us first

If there is any possibility a UK coroner may need to hold an inquest, cremation abroad is not an option. Cremation is irreversible and destroys evidence that a post-mortem examination would require. If you are unsure, contact us via our enquiry form or WhatsApp before proceeding.
