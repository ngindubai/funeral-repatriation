Privacy policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

What we collect

When you submit a contact form, we collect your name, email address, phone number (if provided), and the details of your enquiry. We do not collect data through cookies or tracking scripts beyond basic privacy-respecting analytics.

How we use it

Your information is used only to respond to your enquiry and, if requested, to coordinate repatriation services. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to third parties.

How long we keep it

Enquiry data is kept for up to 12 months after last contact, then deleted. If you request deletion sooner, we will comply within 30 days.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at any time to exercise these rights.

Analytics

We use privacy-respecting analytics that do not use cookies or track individual visitors. No personal data is collected through analytics.
