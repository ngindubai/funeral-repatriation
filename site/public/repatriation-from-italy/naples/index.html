The process

Repatriation from Naples: how it works

The process for repatriation from Naples follows the standard Italy procedure. A local funeral director handles embalming, documentation, and the zinc-lined coffin requirement. Cargo flies from NAP to the UK. Typical timeline is 10-21 days, cost GBP 3,000-8,000.

Amalfi Coast road deaths are a recurring pattern. Capri and island access complications. Southern Italy bureaucracy.
