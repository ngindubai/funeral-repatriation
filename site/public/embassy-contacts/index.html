Consular assistance

British Embassy contacts: when someone dies abroad

Embassy and consulate contact details for British families dealing with a death abroad. Includes the FCDO 24-hour emergency line, main embassy numbers, and what consular staff can and cannot do.

FCDO 24/7: +44 (0)20 7008 5000

In any emergency: call the FCDO 24-hour line on +44 (0)20 7008 5000 from the UK, or contact the nearest British Embassy or Consulate in the country where the death occurred. All numbers below should be verified at gov.uk before travel.
