Cost

What does repatriation from {{ $c.name }} cost?

Every case is different, so we do not quote a figure until we know the details. Contact us for a case-specific estimate with no obligation.

What the cost depends on

  • Whether a post-mortem or inquest is required
  • Distance and location, including island or remote transfers
  • Documentation and certification needed for {{ $c.name }}
  • Whether travel insurance covers repatriation
  • Air freight availability and timing

Travel insurance with repatriation cover often meets the full cost. Without insurance, the family is responsible for costs directly; the embassy and consulate cannot pay repatriation expenses. How we verify our information.

Travel insurance

Check whether your policy, or the deceased's policy, includes repatriation cover. If it does, the insurer typically appoints and funds the funeral director directly.
