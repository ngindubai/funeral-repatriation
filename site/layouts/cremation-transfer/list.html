Cremation abroad and ashes transport to the UK

For some families, having their loved one cremated in the country where they died, and carrying the ashes home personally, is the right choice. It is often simpler and less costly than full body repatriation. These guides cover the process, documentation, and airline rules for each country.

Before you decide: speak to us first

If there is any possibility a UK coroner may need to hold an inquest, cremation abroad is not an option. Cremation is irreversible and destroys evidence that a post-mortem examination would require. If you are unsure, call our 24/7 line on {{ site.Params.emergencyPhone }} before proceeding.
