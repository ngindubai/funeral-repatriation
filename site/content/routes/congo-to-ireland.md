    answer: "There is no resident Irish Embassy in the Republic of Congo. Consular cover is provided by the Irish Embassy in Abuja, Nigeria. Contact the Department of Foreign Affairs 24-hour emergency line on +353 1 408 2000 (gov.ie, 2026) and they will direct you to the relevant consular officer."
