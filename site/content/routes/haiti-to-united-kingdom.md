  - question: "What documents are required for repatriation from Haiti?"
    answer: "The core documents are: Acte de décès (état civil death certificate) with certified English translation, embalming certificate, freedom from infection certificate, and the export permit. Your repatriation coordinator manages all of this on your behalf."
  - question: "Does the British Embassy help with repatriation from Haiti?"
    answer: "The British Embassy in Kingston, Jamaica, covers Haiti for consular purposes and can register the death with UK authorities and provide whatever guidance is possible. They cannot pay for or arrange repatriation, and direct operational access into Haiti is constrained by security conditions. The FCDO 24-hour emergency line is +44 (0)20 7008 5000."
