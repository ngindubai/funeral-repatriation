A death that is violent, suspicious or unexplained falls under the jurisdiction of the Police and magistrate, which may require a post-mortem first. With the paperwork complete, the body is embalmed, sealed in a zinc-lined coffin, and cleared for export before being flown to New Zealand as air cargo. A funeral director in New Zealand then takes custody. The full process most often takes 2-3 weeks."
