Armed groups and the Russian Wagner Group (Africa Corps) hold significant influence outside the capital. All documentation from Norway must be in order before the body is released for the funeral."

A case is only workable where the death has occurred in the capital, and even then it can involve MINUSCA, the UN peacekeeping mission, alongside national authorities."
