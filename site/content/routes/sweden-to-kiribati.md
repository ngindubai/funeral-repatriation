    answer: "The funeral director in Kiribati takes custody at the cargo terminal at Bonriki International Airport (TRW), South Tarawa.   - question: "Does it matter which of Kiribati's island groups the death occurred in, for a family in Sweden?"

There is no political travel advisory here; the complexity is purely a function of geography."
