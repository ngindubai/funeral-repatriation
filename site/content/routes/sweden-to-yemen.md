    answer: "Reception depends entirely on which authority controls the area of arrival. Aden International Airport (ADE), under the internationally recognised government, is the more accessible gateway; Sana'a International Airport (SAH), Houthi-controlled, has very limited and unpredictable scheduled service. All documentation from Sweden must be in order before the body is released for the funeral."

    answer: "Yes. Cremation in Sweden is an option in most cases, though local authorities must release the body before cremation can take place. Ask our team for specific guidance on your case."
  - question: "Does it make a difference to a family in Sweden which part of Yemen the death occurred in?"
    answer: "Yes, fundamentally. Documentation, and even whether repatriation is achievable at all, depends on which authority controls the location of death. Aden International Airport under the internationally recognised government is the more accessible gateway; Sana'a's airport has very limited, unpredictable service under Houthi control. The FCDO advises against all travel to Yemen, and any case here should begin with a call to the FCDO emergency line before contacting a specialist firm."
