overview_body: "Repatriation from Netherlands begins at the local level: contacting the emergency services on 112, then registering the death with the Burgerlijke Stand (Civil Registry) and obtaining a certified local death certificate, which usually takes 3-7 days. A death that is violent, suspicious or unexplained falls under the jurisdiction of the Officier van Justitie (Public Prosecutor), which may require a post-mortem first. With the paperwork complete, the body is embalmed, sealed in a zinc-lined coffin, and cleared for export before being flown to South Africa as air cargo. A funeral director in South Africa then takes custody. The full process most often takes 1-2 weeks."
