    answer: "The funeral director in Timor-Leste takes custody at the cargo terminal at Presidente Nicolau Lobato International Airport (DIL), Dili.   - question: "What happens if the death in Timor-Leste occurs in the Oecusse enclave, for a family in Norway?"

Reaching it by road means crossing into Indonesian territory and back out again; the alternative is a charter flight to the enclave's own small airstrip. Either way, a case in Oecusse takes noticeably longer to reach Dili, where the Civil Registration Service and the Australian Embassy's consular support are based, than a death in the capital itself."
