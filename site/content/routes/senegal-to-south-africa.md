Obtaining the local death certificate typically takes 7-14 days, and it is this document that lets the rest of the process begin. If the death is treated as violent, suspicious or unexplained, the Police and Prosecutor takes jurisdiction, and a post-mortem may be needed before release. After the documentation is in order, the body is embalmed and placed in a zinc-lined coffin for international transport, then sent to South Africa by air cargo. From death to arrival in South Africa, most cases take 2-4 weeks."
