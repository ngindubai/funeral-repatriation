    answer: "The funeral director in the Republic of Congo takes custody at the cargo terminal at Maya-Maya Airport (BZV), Brazzaville, or Pointe-Noire Airport (PNR), depending on where the death occurred.   - question: "Why is repatriation from Canada to Congo covered by an embassy in a different country?"
    answer: "The British Embassy in Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of Congo, provides non-resident consular cover for the Republic of Congo, even though the two countries are entirely separate: different governments, different legal systems, sharing only the Congo River as a border. The Republic of Congo is not a Hague Apostille Convention member, so documents from Canada require standard legalisation rather than a simpler apostille stamp."
