overview_body: "Repatriation from Romania begins at the local level: contacting the emergency services on 112, then registering the death with the Stare Civila (Civil Status Office) and obtaining a certified local death certificate, which usually takes 3-7 days. A death that is violent, suspicious or unexplained falls under the jurisdiction of the Prosecutor's Office, which may require a post-mortem first. With the paperwork complete, the body is embalmed, sealed in a zinc-lined coffin, and cleared for export before being flown to Germany as air cargo. A funeral director in Germany then takes custody. The full process most often takes 1-2 weeks."
