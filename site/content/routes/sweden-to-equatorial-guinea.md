    answer: "The funeral director in Equatorial Guinea takes custody at the cargo terminal at Malabo Airport (SSG) on Bioko Island. Equatorial Guinea is not a Hague Apostille Convention member; documents require legalisation. Documentation is issued in Spanish. Deaths on the Rio Muni mainland require internal transfer to Malabo before international repatriation can proceed. All documentation from Sweden must be in order before the body is released for the funeral."
