Brazil repatriation cases can be smooth in major cities but slower where document flow and transfers are fragmented.

## Timeline expectation

Many straightforward cases complete in 12 to 24 days. Complex cases can exceed this when multiple agencies are involved.

## Main risk points

- Certificate sequence errors
- Delays in official legalisation steps
- Airport transfer timing from non-hub locations

## Practical checklist

1. Use a local provider with international repatriation track record.
2. Keep translated names consistent with passport spelling.
3. Track each required certificate as a checklist, not by memory.
4. Keep UK receiving team active from day one.

When the document chain is controlled carefully, most delays become predictable.
