Repatriation from Mexico is usually achievable within a practical timeline when local documentation is handled early and accurately.

## Typical timeline

Straightforward cases often complete in 10 to 21 days. Cases with investigation, post-mortem backlog, or remote transfer can take longer.

## Key process points

1. Confirm local death registration path in the state where death occurred.
2. Secure medical and release documents before cargo booking.
3. Keep all name fields identical to passport records.
4. Coordinate UK receiving funeral director before departure.

## Common delay points

- Translation and certification queues
- Inconsistent names across Spanish and English paperwork
- Delays moving remains from non-hub locations

Families who appoint an experienced local funeral director early usually avoid most preventable delay.
