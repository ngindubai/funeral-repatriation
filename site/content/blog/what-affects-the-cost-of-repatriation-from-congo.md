The Republic of Congo, often called Congo-Brazzaville to keep it separate from its much larger neighbour, is a country where the documentation itself is reasonably well established but where geography and security still shape every case.

## Consular support comes from across the river

There's no British Embassy here. UK consular assistance comes from the British Embassy in Kinshasa, in the Democratic Republic of Congo, which sits directly opposite Brazzaville on the far bank of the Congo River. It's a functioning, well-understood arrangement rather than a genuine gap, but it does add a coordination step that a country with its own resident Embassy wouldn't need.

## Two cities, one working system

Brazzaville and Pointe-Noire both have working civil registration through the Tribunal de Grande Instance, and the Parquet, the state prosecutor's office, has to issue clearance before remains can be released. Outside those two cities, particularly in the northern Likouala and Sangha departments, documentation infrastructure is much thinner, and getting certificates can mean significant travel to a provincial centre.

## Why the Pool department is different

The FCDO advises against all but essential travel to the Pool department, south of Brazzaville, where militia activity, historically linked to the Ninja movement, has continued in sporadic incidents. A death here isn't impossible to manage, but it needs specialist coordination and realistic expectations about timing from the outset.

## No cremation, so repatriation is the only route

Cremation facilities don't exist in the Republic of Congo. For any family who wants cremation rather than local burial, full repatriation to the UK is the only path available, which makes getting the documentation chain moving early even more important.

## Getting home

Air France operates direct flights to Paris from both Brazzaville's Maya-Maya Airport and Pointe-Noire Airport, with onward connections to the UK. Turkish Airlines via Istanbul and Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa are the other realistic options depending on which city the case starts from.
