Rwanda is a small, densely administered country with a reputation for efficiency that extends to its government services. For repatriation purposes, it is consistently one of the faster East African processes. A direct RwandAir service from Kigali to London Heathrow removes the hub connection that complicates most other East African cases.

British visitors come primarily for gorilla trekking in the Volcanoes National Park, and for Kigali itself, which has become a significant conference and business destination.

## Rwanda National Police investigation

All sudden, unnatural, or unexplained deaths in Rwanda are investigated by the Rwanda National Police (RNP). The RNP investigates and refers forensic cases to the Central Police Hospital in Kigali or to University Teaching Hospital of Kigali (CHUK) for post-mortem. The RNP must formally close the investigation and issue clearance before the Ministry of Health processing can proceed.

Rwanda's small geographic size — 26,338 square kilometres — means that transfers from anywhere in the country to Kigali are relatively short. Even deaths in the Volcanoes National Park, in north-western Rwanda near the border with DRC, can reach Kigali within a few hours. This is a meaningful practical advantage over larger East African neighbours.

## Volcanoes National Park and gorilla trekking

Deaths in Volcanoes National Park during gorilla trekking require notification to the Rwanda Development Board (RDB), which manages the park. RDB clearance is required before the body can leave the national park. Kigali International Airport is approximately 100 kilometres from the park entrance. Given Rwanda's road infrastructure, transfer is typically under 3 hours.

## Documentation

Rwanda's official languages are Kinyarwanda, English, and French. Government documentation is issued in English and French. For UK purposes, no translation is required where documentation is already in English — an administrative advantage over most African repatriation cases.

## British Embassy

The British Embassy is in Kigali. Emergency number: +44 20 7008 5000.

## RwandAir and the direct London route

RwandAir operates direct Kigali–London Heathrow services. This is the defining logistical advantage for Rwanda repatriation: the body does not require a hub connection in Nairobi, Addis Ababa, or Dubai. RwandAir handles human remains cargo; the airline has specific documentation requirements, and booking must go through the cargo division. Kigali International Airport (KGL) is the sole international airport.

If RwandAir is unsuitable for scheduling reasons, Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa and Kenya Airways via Nairobi are the standard alternatives.

## Timelines

Kigali, natural death: 10 to 15 days.
Standard case with RNP investigation: 14 to 21 days.
Volcanoes National Park with RDB clearance: add 1 to 2 days.
