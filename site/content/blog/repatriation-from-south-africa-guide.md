South Africa routes can be efficient from major cities, but distance and paperwork quality still drive outcomes.

## Typical timeline

Straightforward city cases often move in 10 to 18 days. Rural transfer and investigation cases can extend further.

## Documentation focus

- Death registration and release documents
- Embalming and transport preparation certification
- Airline cargo acceptance documentation

## Route reality

Johannesburg routes are usually easier for onward cargo planning than smaller locations. Early logistics planning reduces rework.

## Family actions that help

1. Keep one family contact as decision lead.
2. Confirm all personal details line by line before submission.
3. Avoid fixing a funeral date before confirmed cargo movement.

Clear process ownership at both ends usually delivers a smoother repatriation.
