One spelling difference can stop movement for days. Name mismatch is one of the most preventable delay causes in repatriation.

## Where mismatch usually appears

- Passport vs death certificate
- Transliteration differences in translated documents
- Middle names dropped on one form but not another

## Impact

Airlines, authorities, and funeral directors can reject or pause documents until correction is completed.

## Prevention checklist

1. Use passport spelling as the master reference.
2. Check every new document against that reference.
3. Confirm transliteration consistency across translations.
4. Correct errors before cargo booking submission.

Small detail control saves large timeline loss.
