The right funeral director reduces stress, delay, and document errors. The wrong one adds all three.

## Questions to ask first

- How many repatriations have you handled from this country in the last year?
- Which local partner handles documents on the ground?
- What is your typical timeline for similar cases?
- What is included in your quote and what is excluded?

## Signs of a reliable operator

- Clear written quote
- Clear document checklist
- Single named case manager
- Willingness to explain delays transparently

## Warning signs

- Vague pricing
- No country-specific experience
- Promises of guaranteed timelines
- Poor response quality in the first 24 hours

Families usually get the best outcome when they choose based on track record and process clarity, not only on headline price.
