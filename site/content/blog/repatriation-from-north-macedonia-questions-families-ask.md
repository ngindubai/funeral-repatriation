North Macedonia tends to be one of the more manageable Balkan repatriations, and the reasons are fairly specific: a resident British Embassy in Skopje, competent forensic medicine services, and reasonably affordable logistics throughout.

## What decides the timeline

A natural death with complete documentation typically clears in ten to fourteen days. Where the Institute of Forensic Medicine, based at Ss. Cyril and Methodius University in Skopje, has to get involved, that stretches to fourteen to twenty-one days. The Institute handles every non-natural death and any natural death where the cause isn't clear, and it operates to a fair standard for the region. Ministry of Interior clearance has to come through before a funeral director can proceed, usually three to five days for a straightforward accident, longer for a more complex investigation.

## Ohrid changes the picture in summer

Ohrid Lake, one of the oldest lakes in Europe and a UNESCO World Heritage site, draws serious numbers of UK tourists through TUI and specialist Balkan operators each summer. A death there during the season is registered through the local municipal civil registry, and Ohrid Airport's seasonal charter flights, some running direct to UK regional airports, can keep the case moving at roughly the same pace as one in Skopje. Outside summer, when the airport's schedule thins out, everything routes through Skopje instead.

## The Cyrillic question

Official documents come out in Macedonian, written in Cyrillic script, and UK authorities require certified English translation before they'll accept any of it. In the west of the country, particularly around Tetovo and Gostivar, Albanian is also widely used, and documents from those municipalities can appear in both languages, which sometimes means arranging a second translation.

## Mountain regions

Mavrovo National Park, Sar Mountain, and Pelister Mountain attract serious hikers, and any death there, a fall, a medical emergency, hypothermia, needs Mountain Rescue North Macedonia to reach the body before the standard process can start. Build in one to three extra days for that stage alone.

Skopje (SKP) or Ohrid (OHD) connect onward via Belgrade, Vienna, or Istanbul to London.
