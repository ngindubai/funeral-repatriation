Burkina Faso sits among the more demanding repatriation cases in West Africa, not because the underlying documentation process is unusual, but because of what surrounds it: no resident Embassy, a junta government, and active armed group presence across large parts of the country.

## No Embassy on the ground

There has been no resident British Embassy in Burkina Faso since before the current government took power. The British High Commission in Accra, Ghana, handles every consular matter for British nationals in Burkina Faso, coordinating death registration and family liaison from outside the country rather than through a local post. This remote coordination is standard practice for this corridor, not a temporary gap.

## Governance since the 2022 coup

Captain Ibrahim Traoré's CNSP has controlled Burkina Faso's institutions since September 2022. The underlying legal framework hasn't changed: death registration still runs through the état civil, and the Tribunal de Grande Instance still authorises release in non-natural deaths. What has changed is predictability. Judicial and ministerial processes under military administration can move at a different pace than the same steps would under a civilian government, and a specialist firm with current Ouagadougou contacts matters more here than in most corridors.

## Where FCDO advice draws the line

Northern, eastern, and western border regions carry an advise-against-all-travel warning, reflecting JNIM and ISWAP-affiliated activity in those areas. The rest of the country, including Ouagadougou, sits under an advise-against-all-but-essential-travel warning. Body recovery from the advised-against regions isn't achievable through ordinary civilian channels, and this is a real constraint on what a case can accomplish, not a formality.

## Getting a body out of Ouagadougou

Ethiopian Airlines connecting through Addis Ababa is currently the most dependable option. Royal Air Maroc via Casablanca and Brussels Airlines offer alternative routings. Air France's direct Ouagadougou service is irregular enough that it needs checking before any cargo booking relies on it.
