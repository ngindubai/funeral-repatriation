Cremation does not occur in Libya. Libya is a predominantly Sunni Muslim country with no cremation tradition and no cremation facilities. A cremation transfer from Libya to the UK is not possible.

All repatriations from Libya involve body transfer — a process of extreme complexity given the absence of a resident British Embassy, active armed conflict across significant parts of the country, and limited civil infrastructure.

If you need to repatriate a loved one from Libya, please read our [repatriation from Libya](/repatriation-from-libya/) page for full details on what the process actually involves. Call us immediately — Libya cases require specialist coordination and early engagement is essential.
