There are no cremation facilities in Cape Verde. A cremation transfer from Cape Verde is not possible — the body must be repatriated to the UK first. Cremation in the UK can then be arranged through a UK funeral director following Coroner release.

If you are planning to repatriate a loved one from Cape Verde with the intention of cremation in the UK, please see our [repatriation from Cape Verde](/repatriation-from-cape-verde/) guide for full details on the body repatriation process, including inter-island transfers.

For advice on transporting ashes from a prior UK cremation to Cape Verde for a memorial, contact us directly — this is possible under IATA Packing Instruction 650 as accompanied baggage.
