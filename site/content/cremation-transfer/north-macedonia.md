Cremation facilities are available in Skopje. Where a family chooses cremation in North Macedonia rather than body repatriation, the process requires: the Izvod od maticen (death certificate) from the municipal register, Institute of Forensic Medicine clearance and Ministry of Interior confirmation for non-natural deaths, a cremation certificate from the Skopje facility, and a Ministry of Health export licence.

All documentation is in Macedonian Cyrillic script and requires certified English translation. The British Embassy in Skopje handles consular registration.

Ashes are routed to the UK via Belgrade, Vienna, or Istanbul to London as cargo or accompanied baggage under IATA Packing Instruction 650.
