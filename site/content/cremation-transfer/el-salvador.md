Cremation facilities exist in San Salvador. Where a family chooses cremation in El Salvador rather than body repatriation, the process requires: the Partida de Defuncion from the civil registry, IML forensic release and FGR clearance for non-natural deaths, a cremation certificate from the licensed facility, and a Ministry of Health export permit.

All documentation is in Spanish and requires certified English translation. The Honorary Consul in San Salvador and British Embassy Guatemala City handle consular registration.

Ashes typically travel to the UK as accompanied baggage via US hubs (Miami, Houston, Dallas) to London, complying with IATA Packing Instruction 650.
