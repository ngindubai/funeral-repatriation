## What we do

We coordinate the repatriation of loved ones who have died abroad back to the United Kingdom. Our team works directly with local funeral directors, embassies, airlines, and UK funeral homes to handle every step of the process.

### Full body repatriation

From death registration and embalming through to airport transfer and arrival at a UK funeral director of your choice. We coordinate every step so you do not have to navigate foreign systems alone.

### Ashes transport

If cremation abroad is preferred, we coordinate local cremation and arrange transport of ashes back to the UK. This is often simpler and less costly than full body repatriation.

### Cremation abroad

Some families choose to hold a cremation ceremony in the country where their loved one died, particularly when there are cultural or religious reasons to do so. We can coordinate this with local funeral directors.

### Guidance and support

Not everyone needs full coordination. Our country guides provide free, detailed, step-by-step instructions. If you just need answers to specific questions, contact us and we will help where we can.
