The British Embassy in Skopje is the primary UK consular post for North Macedonia. The Embassy handles consular death registration, liaison with Macedonian authorities including the Institute of Forensic Medicine and the Ministry of Interior, and support for families repatriating a loved one.

**Emergency contact (24 hours):** FCDO Emergency Travel Line: +44 207 008 5000

**British Embassy Skopje:** +389 2 3299 299

The Embassy is experienced with repatriation cases from both Skopje and the Ohrid Lake area. For summer tourist season deaths at Ohrid, the Embassy has established working relationships with local authorities and the Institute of Forensic Medicine. The Embassy does not fund repatriation but provides consular registration and can signpost to local funeral directors.
