The British Embassy in Tripoli closed in 2014. There is no resident British Embassy in Libya. Consular services for British nationals in Libya are handled remotely by British Embassy Tunis (Tunisia).

**FCDO Emergency Travel Line (24 hours):** +44 207 008 5000

**British Embassy Tunis:** +216 71 108 700

FCDO advises against all travel to Libya. If you need to contact UK consular services regarding a death in Libya, call the FCDO Emergency Travel Line first. They will initiate contact with British Embassy Tunis and advise on the correct procedure given the current situation on the ground.

Do not attempt to travel to Libya to assist. UK consular protection is unavailable in-country. All Libya cases require specialist coordination — see our [repatriation from Libya](/repatriation-from-libya/) guide for full details.
