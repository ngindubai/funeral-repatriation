The British Embassy in Luanda is the primary UK consular post for Angola. The Embassy handles consular death registration, liaison with Angolan authorities including the Criminal Investigation Service (SIC), and support for families repatriating a loved one.

**Emergency contact (24 hours):** FCDO Emergency Travel Line: +44 207 008 5000

**British Embassy Luanda:** +244 222 334 582

For repatriation cases, the Embassy works alongside licensed Angolan funeral directors and UK-based repatriation specialists. The Embassy does not arrange or fund repatriation directly but provides consular registration and can provide lists of local English-speaking funeral directors on request.
