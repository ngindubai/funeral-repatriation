El Salvador does not have a resident British Embassy. Consular services are provided by the Honorary Consul in San Salvador for initial registration and family liaison, with formal consular authority held by British Embassy Guatemala City, which is accredited to El Salvador.

**Emergency contact (24 hours):** FCDO Emergency Travel Line: +44 207 008 5000

**Honorary Consul, San Salvador:** Contact via FCDO for current details

**British Embassy Guatemala City:** +502 2380 7300

For repatriation cases, the Honorary Consul handles initial contact and document witnessing. Formal death registration and repatriation authorisation requires British Embassy Guatemala City. Contacting the FCDO Emergency Travel Line first ensures both offices are notified simultaneously.
