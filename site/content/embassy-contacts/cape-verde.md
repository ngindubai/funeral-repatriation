Cape Verde does not have a resident British Embassy. Consular services for British nationals in Cape Verde are provided by the Honorary Consul in Praia (Santiago Island) and, for formal registration and complex cases, by British Embassy Dakar (Senegal), which is accredited to Cape Verde.

**Emergency contact (24 hours):** FCDO Emergency Travel Line: +44 207 008 5000

**Honorary Consul, Praia:** +238 260 3040

**British Embassy Dakar:** +221 33 823 7392

The Honorary Consul handles initial death registration and family liaison. Formal consular registration and repatriation authorisation is managed by British Embassy Dakar. In all cases, contacting the FCDO Emergency Travel Line first ensures the correct consular post is activated without delay.
