Cremation facilities exist in San Salvador. If your loved one was cremated in El Salvador, bringing the ashes to the UK requires a cremation certificate from the Salvadoran funeral home, a Ministry of Health export clearance, and compliance with IATA Packing Instruction 650 for cargo or airline cabin rules for hand luggage.

All documentation is in Spanish and requires certified English translation. The Honorary Consul in San Salvador, supported by British Embassy Guatemala City, handles consular registration.

Ashes typically travel as accompanied baggage. Contact us for guidance on the specific documentation required for ashes repatriation from El Salvador.
