Cremation facilities are available in Skopje. If your loved one was cremated in North Macedonia, bringing their ashes to the UK requires a cremation certificate from the Macedonian funeral director, a Ministry of Health export clearance, and standard IATA Packing Instruction 650 compliance for cargo shipment.

Documentation is in Macedonian (Cyrillic script) and requires certified English translation. The British Embassy in Skopje handles consular registration.

Ashes typically travel via Belgrade, Vienna, or Istanbul to London on the same routing as body repatriations from North Macedonia. Contact us for guidance on the specific documentation and routing for your case.
