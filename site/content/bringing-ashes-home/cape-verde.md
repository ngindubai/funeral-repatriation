There are no cremation facilities anywhere in the Cape Verde archipelago. If you are hoping to have your loved one cremated in Cape Verde and then bring the ashes home, this is not possible. The body must be repatriated to the UK, and cremation can be arranged through a UK funeral director after Coroner release.

If you wish to bring ashes from a UK cremation to Cape Verde for a memorial or scattering, this is possible. Ashes transported as hand luggage must comply with IATA Packing Instruction 650 — a sealed, sift-proof container with a cremation certificate — and must comply with the airline's own cabin baggage rules.
