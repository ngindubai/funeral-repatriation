Cremation facilities exist in Luanda, though capacity is limited. If your loved one was cremated in Angola and you need to bring their ashes back to the UK, the process involves Angolan Ministry of Health clearance and standard IATA packaging requirements.

All documentation is in Portuguese and requires certified English translation before UK import. The British Embassy in Luanda can assist with consular registration of the original death.
