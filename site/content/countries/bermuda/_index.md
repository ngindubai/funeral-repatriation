## Repatriation from Bermuda to the UK

Bermuda is a British Overseas Territory located in the North Atlantic Ocean, approximately 1,700 km east of the United States. It is the world's most northerly tropical island group. Hamilton is the capital. Bermuda is known for its pink sand beaches and is a major international insurance and reinsurance centre.

As a British Overseas Territory, the Crown's interests are represented by the Governor. Consular functions for UK nationals are handled via the FCO in London. Contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours) to register a death and obtain consular guidance.

### The Legal Framework

Death registration is handled by the **Registrar General's Office** in Hamilton, under the Government of Bermuda. English common law applies. Non-natural deaths — accidents, suspected criminal involvement — require investigation and clearance by the **Bermuda Police Service** before funeral arrangements proceed.

All documentation is in English.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate | Registrar General's Office, Government of Bermuda |
| Police Service clearance | Bermuda Police Service (non-natural deaths) |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local funeral director |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Bermuda health authority |
| Export permit | Government of Bermuda |
| UK Coroner notification | FCO / HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing to the UK

**L.F. Wade International Airport (BDA)** near Hamilton has direct **British Airways** service to London Gatwick (LGW). Flight time is approximately 7 hours. Additional connections to New York, Boston, and Toronto are available for alternative routings if needed.

### Cremation

Cremation is available in Bermuda. Ashes may be returned to the UK in a sealed urn.
