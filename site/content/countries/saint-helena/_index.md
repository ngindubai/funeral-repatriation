Saint Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha is a British Overseas Territory comprising three islands in the South Atlantic. English common law applies across all three, with local governance on each island.

Saint Helena gained its first airport in 2016 after centuries of ship-only access. Flight frequency remains very limited. Ascension is a military support station. Tristan da Cunha has no air connection whatsoever — the nearest inhabited land is St Helena, 1,500 miles away.
