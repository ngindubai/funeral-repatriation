Guam is a US organised territory at the western edge of the Pacific Ocean, approximately 8,000 miles from the UK and 1,500 miles east of the Philippines. US federal law governs body export. The Medical Examiner has jurisdiction for all non-natural deaths.

The British Embassy in Tokyo covers consular matters for Guam on a non-resident basis. There is no direct UK flight from Antonio B. Won Pat International Airport — all repatriation routing requires transit through Tokyo or Honolulu.
