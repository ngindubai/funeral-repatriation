French Guiana is an overseas department and region of France on the north-east coast of South America. It is legally part of France — the same civil law that governs Paris governs Cayenne. The British Embassy in Paris is the relevant consular post.

Interior jungle regions are vast and have limited infrastructure. Gold-mining areas near the Suriname and Brazil borders carry security risks. Any death in the interior requires getting the body to Cayenne first, which adds time.
