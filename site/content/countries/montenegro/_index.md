## Repatriation from Montenegro: What Families Need to Know

Montenegro has become one of the fastest-growing British holiday destinations in the Western Balkans. The Bay of Kotor, Budva Riviera, and Tivat marina attract substantial British tourist volumes each summer. TUI and Jet2 operate direct charter flights from UK airports to Tivat (TIV) during the season.

Those charter flights are useful for passengers. They are irrelevant for repatriation cargo. Human remains must route via a full-service scheduled carrier.

Montenegro is an EU candidate state but has not yet joined the EU. It has not signed the Strasbourg Convention. The full consular processing pathway applies.

### The British Embassy Podgorica

The British Embassy in Podgorica covers the whole of Montenegro. Staff have experience with British national deaths given the volume of UK coastal tourists. Contact the Embassy emergency line immediately following a death.

### Forensic Process

The Institut za sudsku medicinu (Institute for Forensic Medicine) at the Faculty of Medicine, University of Montenegro in Podgorica, conducts post-mortems for unnatural or suspicious deaths. This is the sole national forensic facility. Deaths anywhere in Montenegro — coastal, mountain, or urban — transfer to Podgorica for forensic post-mortem if required.

### Bay of Kotor, Budva, Tivat

The main British tourist belt runs from the Bay of Kotor (Kotor, Perast, Risan) south to Budva and Tivat. Deaths in this coastal zone are handled by local police and the nearest municipal medical facility before transfer to Podgorica. The coastal police are familiar with tourist deaths.

Drowning and swimming-related deaths are the most common British tourist fatalities on the Montenegrin coast.

### Durmitor National Park

Durmitor is a UNESCO World Heritage mountain area in northeast Montenegro, about three hours from Podgorica. Bobotov Kuk (2523m) attracts trekkers. The Tara Canyon (Europe's deepest canyon) has rafting and adventure tourism.

Deaths in Durmitor require mountain rescue involvement (Gorska Sluzba Spasavanja). The nearest town is Zabljak, which has basic medical facilities. Complex forensic cases transfer to Podgorica. Road access to Durmitor from the coast takes approximately three hours.

### Documentation Chain

1. Izvod iz maticne knjige umrlih (death certificate) from the Maticni ured (registry office).
2. Police investigation closure for unnatural deaths.
3. Forensic post-mortem report if required.
4. Ministry of Health export permit.
5. Certified English translations of Montenegrin documents.
6. Embalming certificate.

Montenegrin uses both Cyrillic and Latin scripts. Certified translators must be accredited in Montenegrin legal language, not simply Serbian or Croatian.

### Routing

Podgorica Airport (TGD) is the main international hub. Tivat Airport (TIV) serves the coastal resort area but has limited scheduled services and no cargo capacity for human remains. Cargo routes via Belgrade (BEG) on Air Serbia, connecting to London Heathrow or Gatwick via Western European hubs. Turkish Airlines via Istanbul (IST) is an alternative.
