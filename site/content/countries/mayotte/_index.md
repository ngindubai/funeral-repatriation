Mayotte became France's 101st department in 2011 and is the easternmost French territory, situated in the Comoros archipelago in the Indian Ocean between Madagascar and the African mainland. French civil law applies in full. The population is approximately 320,000 and is predominantly Muslim.

The airport handles only narrowbody jets, which means there is no direct connection to mainland France. All routing for repatriation goes via Réunion or Madagascar before connecting to Paris CDG. The British Embassy in Paris handles consular matters on a non-resident basis.
