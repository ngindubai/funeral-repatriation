## Repatriation from Mauritius: What Families Need to Know

Mauritius is among the less complicated international repatriations for UK families. English is an official language. There are direct flights to London. The British High Commission in Port Louis has substantial experience handling British national deaths given the very high volume of UK tourists — approximately 150,000 British visitors travel to Mauritius each year (Mauritius Tourism Promotion Authority, 2024).

The process follows a clear path. Most cases that are straightforward — natural deaths at a hotel or hospital — resolve in under three weeks.

### The British High Commission

As a Commonwealth country, Mauritius hosts a British High Commission rather than an Embassy. The British High Commission Port Louis handles consular cases for British nationals who die in Mauritius. The High Commission team has well-established working relationships with Mauritius Police Force and the Ministry of Health.

Contact the High Commission emergency line as the first priority. They will issue a Certificate of No Impediment to Cremation or advise on the correct documentation pathway.

### Forensic Process

For deaths that are sudden, unexplained, or unnatural, the Mauritius Police Force takes jurisdiction. Post-mortems are conducted at the forensic pathology unit, which operates in conjunction with the SSR National Hospital at Pamplemousses in the north of the island. The Government Forensic Science Laboratory provides supporting analysis.

Drowning and diving deaths are the most common complex cases. Establishing whether a drowning was accidental, whether an underlying medical condition was a factor, or whether there was any third-party involvement takes additional time.

### The Documentation Chain

1. Death certificate from the Civil Status Division.
2. Police investigation report (for unnatural deaths).
3. Forensic post-mortem report if required.
4. Ministry of Health export permit.
5. Embalming certificate.

Most documents are in English, with some in bilingual English/French format.

### Routing

Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam International Airport (MRU) is the only international airport. Both Air Mauritius and British Airways operate nonstop services to London Heathrow. Human remains cargo procedures are well-established with both carriers.
