## Repatriation from Paraguay to the UK

Paraguay is a landlocked South American nation with a functioning civil registration system and an established international airport at Asunción. The British Embassy in Buenos Aires provides non-resident consular coverage. For urgent consular assistance, contact the FCDO on 020 7008 5000 (24 hours). The British Embassy Buenos Aires can be reached on +54 11 4808 2200.

### The Legal Framework

Death registration in Paraguay is managed by the **Dirección General del Registro Civil del Estado Civil de las Personas**. The **Ministerio del Interior** issues the export permit once death certification and health documentation are in place.

In Asunción and the main urban areas, the documentation process is well-established and generally efficient. In the remote Chaco region to the west, and in rural eastern departments, the process may require travel to provincial registration offices.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Certificado de defunción (death certificate) | Dirección General del Registro Civil |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local mortuary |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Ministry of Health |
| Export permit | Ministerio del Interior |
| Consular death registration | British Embassy, Buenos Aires |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

All Spanish-language documents require certified English translation before UK authorities will process them.

### Routing and Transport

Asunción's Silvio Pettirossi International Airport (ASU) is the sole international gateway. Standard routing options:

- **Via Buenos Aires**: Aerolíneas Argentinas and LATAM both connect Asunción to Buenos Aires Ezeiza, from where direct UK flights operate.
- **Via São Paulo**: LATAM connects Asunción to São Paulo Guarulhos, with onward UK connections.
- **Via Miami**: Some routing goes via Miami with American Airlines or LATAM.

Buenos Aires is the most commonly used connecting hub for UK-bound repatriations.

### Geographic Considerations

Paraguay divides into two distinct regions:

- **Eastern Paraguay (Oriental)**: The main population centre, including Asunción, Ciudad del Este, and most major towns. Infrastructure is established.
- **Western Paraguay (Occidental/Chaco)**: Sparsely populated semi-arid region covering roughly 60% of the country. Very limited road infrastructure. Deaths here require specialist ground logistics before documentation can begin.

The **Tri-Border Area** around Ciudad del Este — where Paraguay meets Argentina and Brazil — has elevated organised crime activity. Deaths in this area may involve additional interaction with local law enforcement.

### Cremation

Cremation services are available in Asunción. This is notable for the region. Families who prefer cremation in-country rather than full-body repatriation should discuss this option with a specialist firm.
