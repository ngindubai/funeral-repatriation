## Repatriation from Cook Islands to the UK

Cook Islands is a self-governing state in free association with New Zealand, comprising 15 islands spread across 1.8 million km² of the South Pacific. The capital, Avarua, is on Rarotonga, which also has the main international airport. Cook Islands uses English common law, New Zealand dollar, and documents in English.

Tourism is significant — Cook Islands is a well-established holiday destination, particularly among Australians and New Zealanders. UK nationals also visit, primarily for beaches and diving.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The British High Commission Wellington (+64 4 924 2888) handles consular matters for Cook Islands on a non-resident basis.

### The Legal Framework

Death registration is handled by **Cook Islands Civil Registration** within the Ministry of Internal Affairs. Documentation is in English. Non-natural deaths require **Cook Islands Police Service** investigation and clearance.

### Two Very Different Cases: Rarotonga and the Northern Group

**Rarotonga (Southern Group):** The main island. Well-connected via Air New Zealand and Fiji Airways. Mortuary infrastructure exists. Standard Pacific repatriation case.

**Northern Group atolls (Penrhyn, Manihiki, Rakahanga, Pukapuka, Nassau, Suwarrow):** Some of the Pacific's most remote inhabited islands. Air Rarotonga serves some, but schedules are infrequent. Deaths here require internal transfer to Rarotonga before international repatriation — a process that can take days. Establish the location immediately.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate | Cook Islands Civil Registration, Ministry of Internal Affairs |
| Police clearance | Cook Islands Police Service (non-natural deaths) |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local funeral director |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Health authority |
| Export permit | Relevant authority |
| Consular death registration | BHC Wellington |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing to the UK

**Rarotonga International Airport (RAR)** is served by Air New Zealand (to Auckland) and Fiji Airways (to Nadi). All UK repatriation routing goes via **Auckland (AKL)**, then connecting to UK airports.

### Cremation

Cremation is available at Rarotonga. If chosen, ashes are returned to the UK in a sealed urn.
