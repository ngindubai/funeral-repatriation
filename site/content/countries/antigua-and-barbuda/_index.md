## Repatriation from Antigua and Barbuda: What Families Need to Know

Antigua is one of the UK's best-connected Caribbean destinations. British Airways and Virgin Atlantic both operate direct services from London Gatwick, which gives direct cargo repatriation routing without the inter-island transit required by most Eastern Caribbean islands. This is a practical advantage that reduces both cost and transit time.

There is no resident British diplomatic post in Antigua. An Honorary British Consul operates in St John's. The British High Commission in Bridgetown, Barbados, has formal accreditation.

### Who to Call

1. Honorary British Consul St John's — first consular contact.
2. FCDO Emergency Travel Line: 020 7008 5000 (24 hours).
3. A specialist UK repatriation company.

### Barbuda: A Special Case

Barbuda is a separate island 30 miles north of Antigua. Hurricane Irma, which struck in September 2017, devastated Barbuda entirely. Most of the population was evacuated to Antigua. The island has since been partially resettled but its health infrastructure has not been restored to pre-Irma standards.

For practical purposes, Barbuda has no functioning hospital. The health centre on the island can provide very limited first aid. Any serious medical emergency on Barbuda requires emergency air evacuation to Antigua (15-minute flight). By the time a death occurs on Barbuda, the primary concern has usually already shifted from medical to repatriation — but the evacuation is still required before documentation can begin.

### Mount St John's Medical Centre

Mount St John's Medical Centre (MSJMC) in St John's is the main national hospital and handles forensic post-mortems. It has reasonable capacity by Eastern Caribbean standards, with a permanent consultant pathologist. The coroner process for unnatural deaths follows standard Eastern Caribbean practice.

### British Tourism Profile

Antigua Sailing Week (late April to early May) draws some of the UK's most active sailing community. English Harbour and Falmouth Harbour are the focal points. Nelson's Dockyard National Park is a major heritage attraction. Cricket at the Sir Vivian Richards Stadium has long drawn British cricket supporters.

### Cargo Routing

V.C. Bird International Airport (ANU) handles British Airways and Virgin Atlantic direct services. Cargo can be consigned directly to London Gatwick without inter-island transit — a significant logistics simplification.
