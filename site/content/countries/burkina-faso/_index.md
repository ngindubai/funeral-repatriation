Burkina Faso experienced a military coup in September 2022, when Captain Ibrahim Traoré led the MPSR (Mouvement Patriotique pour la Sauvegarde et la Restauration) to power. The Conseil National pour la Sauvegarde de la Patrie (CNSP) now controls all government institutions.

For UK families, the practical reality is stark: no resident British Embassy, junta-controlled administration, and JNIM/ISWAP-affiliated armed groups active across large parts of the country. Cases in northern, eastern, and western border regions are not achievable.

## Who to contact first

Call the FCDO Emergency Travel Line: **020 7008 5000** (available 24 hours). The British High Commission Accra, Ghana provides non-resident consular coverage for Burkina Faso: +233 302 213 250.

Do not travel to Burkina Faso. FCDO advises against all travel to northern, eastern, and western border regions, and all but essential travel to the rest of the country including Ouagadougou.

## British High Commission Accra: non-resident coverage

All consular services for British nationals in Burkina Faso are handled from the British High Commission in Accra. This includes death registration for UK purposes and liaison with Burkinabé authorities.

## Civil registration and documentation

Burkina Faso uses French civil law. Death registration goes through the état civil (civil status office). For non-natural deaths, the Tribunal de Grande Instance (court of first instance) must issue an order before the death certificate is released. Police Nationale du Burkina Faso must attend and issue clearance.

Under junta administration, judicial and ministerial processes can be delayed. A specialist firm with current Ouagadougou contacts is essential.

## Ministry of Health export permit

Once civil registration and Tribunal clearance are complete, the Ministry of Health issues the export permit. A licensed funeral director embalms and prepares the body. All documents require certified English translation.

## Routing from Ouagadougou

Ouagadougou Airport (OUA):
- Ethiopian Airlines to Addis Ababa (ADD) — primary UK-bound cargo route
- Royal Air Maroc to Casablanca (CMN) — connecting to London
- Air France (OUA) — service irregular, verify before booking
- Brussels Airlines via Brussels

Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa is the most reliable current route for UK-bound cargo.

## Conflict zones: not achievable

FCDO advises against all travel to the Sahel, Nord, Centre-Nord, and Est provinces, border areas with Mali and Niger, and eastern regions of Gourma and Tapoa. Civilian body recovery from these areas is not achievable.
