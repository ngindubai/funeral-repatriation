## Repatriation from Turkmenistan to the UK

Turkmenistan is a Central Asian nation sharing borders with Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Afghanistan, Iran, and the Caspian Sea. It is consistently rated among the world's most repressive and closed states. Despite this, the British Embassy in Ashgabat is resident and provides direct consular support — a significant difference from many other high-complexity destinations.

For urgent consular assistance, contact the FCDO on 020 7008 5000 (24 hours). The British Embassy Ashgabat can be reached on +993 12 363 462.

### The Governance Context

Turkmenistan has been governed by a single-party state since independence in 1991. President Serdar Berdimuhamedow took power in 2022, succeeding his father who ruled from 1985. The country maintains strict controls on the movement of foreign nationals, heavily restricts internet access, and has historically provided limited consular access to detained British nationals. FCDO advises caution in interactions with local authorities.

Bureaucratic processing for foreign nationals — including the steps required for repatriation documentation — can be unpredictable. Specialist local contacts matter significantly in managing these processes.

### The Legal Framework

Death registration in Turkmenistan follows the Soviet model. The **Civil Registry Office (ZAGS)** registers the death and issues the death certificate. The **Ministry of Internal Affairs** issues the export permit once the death certificate, embalming certificate, and freedom from infection certificate are in place.

Documentation is in Turkmen and/or Russian. Certified English translations are required before UK authorities will process any documents.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate | Civil Registry Office (ZAGS) |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local mortuary |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Ministry of Health |
| Export permit | Ministry of Internal Affairs |
| Consular death registration | British Embassy, Ashgabat |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing and Transport

Ashgabat International Airport (ASB) is the sole international gateway. Standard routing:

- **Via Istanbul**: Turkish Airlines operates Ashgabat to Istanbul with UK connections — the primary repatriation route.
- **Via Dubai**: Flydubai serves Ashgabat with connections through Dubai International Airport.

Both routes provide access to UK cargo connections with reasonable reliability.

### Remote and Restricted Areas

Access to the border regions with Afghanistan is severely restricted and requires special permits. The Karakum Desert interior has limited infrastructure. Deaths in these areas require specialist ground logistics and may involve significant delays in accessing the location before documentation can begin.

### Cremation

Cremation is not widely available in Turkmenistan. Repatriation of the body to the UK is the standard approach for UK nationals requiring cremation.
