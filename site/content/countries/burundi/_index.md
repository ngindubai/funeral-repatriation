## Repatriation from Burundi to the UK

Burundi is a small, landlocked East African nation bordering Rwanda, Tanzania, and the Democratic Republic of Congo. The British High Commission in Kampala, Uganda, handles British national consular matters for Burundi on a non-resident basis. French civil law governs civil registration.

For urgent consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The British High Commission Kampala can be reached on +256 31 231 2000.

### The Legal Framework

Death registration in Burundi is handled by the **civil registry (registrar des actes de l'état civil)**. The civil registry issues the official death certificate, which is the foundation for the repatriation process. Where death is not from natural causes, the **Public Prosecutor's Office (Parquet)** must authorise release before the body can be moved.

The Ministry of Interior or relevant authority issues the export permit. Documentation is in Kirundi and/or French, requiring certified English translation.

### Non-Resident Consular Representation

The British High Commission is based in Kampala, not Bujumbura. This means consular death registration involves remote processing. A specialist repatriation firm with local Burundian contacts can manage the documentation process more efficiently than attempting to coordinate remotely from the UK.

### Security Situation

FCDO advises against all but essential travel to some areas of Burundi, particularly border regions. The capital Bujumbura is generally accessible, but the security situation in rural and border areas can change. Confirm current FCDO travel advice for the specific location.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate | Civil registry (registrar des actes de l'état civil) |
| Public Prosecutor's clearance | Parquet (non-natural deaths) |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local mortuary |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Ministry of Health |
| Export permit | Relevant authority |
| Consular death registration | BHC Kampala |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing and Transport

Bujumbura's **Melchior Ndadaye International Airport (BJM)** is the main international gateway. Standard repatriation routing:

- **Via Nairobi**: Kenya Airways and Ethiopian Airlines provide connections to major European hubs.
- **Via Brussels**: Brussels Airlines operates direct services, providing direct access to UK connections.
- **Via Addis Ababa**: Ethiopian Airlines' extensive African network connects to European hubs.

### Cremation

Cremation is not widely available in Burundi. Full body repatriation to the UK is the standard approach.
