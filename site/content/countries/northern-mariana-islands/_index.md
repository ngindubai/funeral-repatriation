The Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands (CNMI) is a US commonwealth territory in the western Pacific, north of Guam. It comprises 14 islands, of which Saipan, Tinian, and Rota are the main inhabited ones. US federal law governs body export from the CNMI.

Saipan International Airport handles the main international connections, predominantly to Tokyo and Guam. The British Embassy in Tokyo covers consular matters on a non-resident basis.
