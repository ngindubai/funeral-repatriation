Mauritania occupies a vast stretch of the Saharan and Sahelian west, from the Atlantic coast to the borders of Mali and Algeria. The capital Nouakchott sits on the Atlantic coast. The interior is almost entirely desert — the country is one of the world's most sparsely populated.

The government is a republic with strong military influence. Civil administration in Nouakchott is functional. There is no resident British Embassy — coverage comes from the British Embassy in Rabat, Morocco.

## Who to contact first

Call the FCDO Emergency Travel Line: **020 7008 5000** (available 24 hours). The British Embassy Rabat, Morocco provides non-resident consular coverage for Mauritania: +212 537 633 333.

FCDO advises against all travel to the Saharan interior and all but essential travel to areas near the Mali border.

## British Embassy Rabat: non-resident coverage

All consular services for British nationals in Mauritania come from the British Embassy in Rabat. The Embassy registers the death for UK purposes and provides guidance on engaging local Mauritanian administrative channels.

## Civil registration

Mauritania uses a legal system based on French civil law for most formal matters, supplemented by Islamic personal status law (Maliki school). Death registration runs through the état civil. Documentation is primarily in Arabic, with French also used in formal administrative contexts.

The attending physician certifies cause of death. The civil registrar issues the death certificate. For non-natural deaths, the Police Nationale must attend and the Parquet (public prosecutor) issues judicial clearance.

## Islamic burial pressure

Mauritania is almost entirely Sunni Muslim. Community expectation strongly favours rapid burial — within 24 hours. Any family intending repatriation must communicate that intent clearly and promptly through local contacts. Early specialist involvement is particularly important.

## Ministry of Health export permit

Once civil registration and police clearance are in hand, the Ministry of Health issues the export permit. A licensed funeral director embalms and prepares the body. Certified English translation of all Arabic and French documents is required.

## Routing from Nouakchott

Nouakchott-Oumtounsy International Airport (NKC):
- Royal Air Maroc to Casablanca (CMN) — primary UK cargo route, connecting London
- Turkish Airlines to Istanbul (IST)
- Air Mauritanie domestic connections

Royal Air Maroc via Casablanca is the most reliable UK-bound cargo pathway from Nouakchott.

## Saharan interior: not accessible

FCDO advises against all travel to the Saharan interior due to risk of kidnap by Al-Qaeda in the Islamic Maghreb (AQIM) and affiliated groups. Body recovery from Saharan interior areas is not achievable.

## What a specialist firm does

A firm with North African and West African Francophone experience manages Police Nationale, Parquet, and Ministry of Health in Nouakchott. Arabic-language documentation capability is essential. Non-resident Embassy coordination via Rabat adds a translation and liaison layer.
