## Repatriation from Réunion to the UK

Réunion is an island in the Indian Ocean approximately 800 km east of Madagascar and 170 km south-west of Mauritius. It is an overseas region and department (région et département d'outre-mer) of the French Republic, and an outermost region of the European Union. The capital is **Saint-Denis** in the north. Réunion is known for its dramatic volcanic landscape, diverse culture, and growing status as a destination for UK long-haul travellers.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The **British Embassy Paris** (+33 1 44 51 31 00) handles consular matters for Réunion on a non-resident basis.

### The Legal Framework

As a French department, Réunion uses the same civil law as metropolitan France. Death registration is through the **mairie** of the commune where the death occurred. Non-natural deaths require investigation by the **Gendarmerie nationale** or **Police nationale**. All documentation is in French.

### Specific Local Hazards

Two hazard types are relevant to circumstances of death in Réunion:

- **Piton de la Fournaise** (active volcano, south-east): The Préfecture issues restricted zone orders during eruptions. Check current FCDO advice and local prefectural bulletins.
- **Shark attacks**: Réunion has a documented shark attack risk, particularly on unprotected beaches outside the western coast lagoon. The local marine reserve authority (ICAD) monitors this.

Neither hazard affects the repatriation process itself but may be relevant to police or coroner investigations in non-natural death cases.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Acte de décès (death certificate) | Mairie of commune of death |
| Certified English translation | Sworn translator |
| Gendarmerie/Police nationale clearance | (Non-natural deaths) |
| Certificat d'embaumement | Licensed local funeral director |
| Certificat de non-contagion | Health authority |
| Autorisation de sortie du territoire | French authorities |
| Consular death registration | British Embassy, Paris |
| UK Coroner permission | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing to the UK

**Roland Garros Airport (RUN)** near Saint-Denis has direct **Air France** service to Paris Charles de Gaulle (CDG), approximately 11 hours. UK connections from CDG are frequent.

### Cremation

Cremation is available in Réunion. Ashes may be returned to the UK in a sealed urn.
