Guinea is a West African country on the Atlantic coast, bordered by Guinea-Bissau, Senegal, Mali, Côte d'Ivoire, Sierra Leone, and Liberia. It has been under military rule since September 2021, when Colonel Mamadi Doumbouya led the CNRD (Comité National du Rassemblement et du Développement) to power.

For UK families, the key facts are: no resident British Embassy, French civil law throughout, and Air France maintaining Conakry service despite the political change.

## Who to contact first

Call the FCDO Emergency Travel Line: **020 7008 5000** (available 24 hours). The British High Commission Freetown, Sierra Leone provides non-resident consular coverage for Guinea: +232 22 232 961.

FCDO advises against all but essential travel to Guinea.

## British High Commission Freetown: non-resident coverage

All consular services for British nationals in Guinea come from the British High Commission in Freetown. This is the standard arrangement for Guinea. The High Commission registers the death for UK purposes and provides guidance on local documentation channels.

## Civil registration

Guinea operates French civil law. Death registration runs through the état civil (civil status office). The attending physician certifies cause of death in French. The civil registrar issues the acte de décès (death certificate) in French.

For non-natural deaths, the Gendarmerie Nationale must attend and issue clearance. The Tribunal de Première Instance issues judicial clearance where an examining magistrate review is required. For straightforward accident cases in Conakry, allow 7 to 14 days for this stage.

## Ministry of Health export permit

Once civil registration and Gendarmerie clearance are complete, the Ministry of Health issues the export permit. A licensed funeral director prepares and embalms the body. All documents require certified English translation before UK authorities can process them.

## Routing from Conakry

Conakry-Gbessia International Airport (CKY):
- Air France to Paris CDG — primary UK cargo route, connecting London
- Royal Air Maroc to Casablanca (CMN)
- Ethiopian Airlines to Addis Ababa (ADD)
- Brussels Airlines via Brussels

Air France maintains scheduled service to Conakry and is the most reliable UK cargo pathway.

## Forested region: elevated risk

The N'Zérékoré area in southeastern Guinea (forested region) experiences periodic inter-communal violence. Access may be restricted following unrest events. For deaths in this region, confirm current security conditions before committing to any timeline.
