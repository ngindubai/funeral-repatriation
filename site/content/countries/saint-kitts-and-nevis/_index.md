## Repatriation from Saint Kitts and Nevis to the UK

Saint Kitts and Nevis is a two-island Commonwealth nation in the Leeward Islands of the Caribbean. English is the official language, and English common law governs civil registration. The federation is a popular tourist destination and a growing financial services centre. British nationals visiting typically need the same repatriation support as across the wider Caribbean — manageable but requiring specialist coordination.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The British High Commission Bridgetown, Barbados (+1 246 430 7800) handles consular matters for Saint Kitts and Nevis on a non-resident basis.

### The Two-Island Consideration

Saint Kitts and Nevis are separate islands with their own airports. **Robert L. Bradshaw International (SKB)** on Saint Kitts handles international flights. **Vance W. Amory International (NEV)** on Nevis handles mainly inter-island and charter flights.

If death occurs on Nevis, the body must be transferred to Saint Kitts via inter-island ferry or small aircraft before international repatriation can begin. A specialist firm will manage this transfer as part of the overall process.

### The Legal Framework

Death registration is handled by the **Registrar General's Office**. The Registrar General issues the official death certificate in English. Where death is not from natural causes, the **Coroner's Court** must issue clearance before the body can be released.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate | Registrar General's Office |
| Coroner's certificate | Coroner's Court (non-natural deaths) |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local mortuary |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Ministry of Health |
| Export permit | Relevant authority |
| Consular death registration | BHC Bridgetown |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing and Transport

Most UK-bound repatriation from Saint Kitts routes via **Antigua (ANU — V.C. Bird International)** or **San Juan (SJU)**, with onward connections to UK airports. American Airlines, Caribbean Airlines, and LIAT connect the islands. Direct UK services operate seasonally.

### Cremation

Cremation is available on Saint Kitts. Ashes repatriation to the UK is a practical alternative to full body repatriation.
