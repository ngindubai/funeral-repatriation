Puerto Rico is a US unincorporated territory in the Caribbean. US federal law governs all aspects of body export — this runs in parallel with the UK Coroner import requirements. UK families must satisfy both.

The Institute of Forensic Sciences in San Juan is the territorial equivalent of a Medical Examiner and takes jurisdiction for all non-natural deaths. The Honorary British Consul in San Juan provides the nearest on-the-ground consular support; the British Embassy Washington DC covers formally.
