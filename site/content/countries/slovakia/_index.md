## Repatriation from Slovakia to the UK

Slovakia is a Central European EU and Schengen member state, bordering Austria, Czech Republic, Hungary, Poland, and Ukraine. The capital is Bratislava, which sits on the Danube just 60 km from Vienna. Slovakia joined the EU in 2004 and adopted the euro in 2009.

A significant number of UK nationals visit Slovakia for tourism (skiing in the High Tatras, historic cities), business, and as part of the wider expat community in Central Europe.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours) or the British Embassy Bratislava directly at +421 2 5998 2000.

### The Legal Framework

Death registration in Slovakia is handled at the local **Matrika** — the civil registry office at municipal level. This produces the official death certificate (Úmrtný list) in Slovak. Where death was not from natural causes, the **Prokuratúra** (Slovak Prosecutor's Office) must authorise release of the body. A specialist repatriation firm will manage this process through local contacts.

### Document Translation

All Slovak documentation requires certified translation into English for the UK Coroner. This is a standard step for repatriations from non-English-speaking EU countries and your specialist firm will arrange it.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate (Úmrtný list) | Matrika (local registry office) |
| Certified English translation | Sworn translator |
| Police / Prokuratúra clearance | Slovak authorities (non-natural deaths) |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed Slovak funeral director |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Health authority |
| Approved sealed coffin documentation | Funeral director |
| Consular death registration | British Embassy, Bratislava |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing to the UK

**Bratislava M.R. Štefánik Airport (BTS)** has direct flights to London operated by Ryanair, Wizz Air, and seasonal carriers. **Vienna International Airport (VIE)** — approximately 1 hour by road — offers a wider choice of UK connections and is commonly used.

**Košice International Airport (KSC)** serves eastern Slovakia with connections via Prague or Vienna.

### Cremation

Cremation is widely available and extensively used in Slovakia. Crematoria operate in Bratislava, Košice, Martin, and other major cities. If cremation is the preferred option, ashes are returned to the UK in a sealed urn.
