Gibraltar is a British Overseas Territory on the southern tip of Spain — two miles square, English-speaking, and governed under English common law. For UK families, it is administratively the closest international repatriation there is.

The HM Coroner for Gibraltar handles all deaths requiring investigation. The Royal Gibraltar Police notify the Coroner for non-natural deaths. Documentation is in English. The flight to London is 2.5 hours.
