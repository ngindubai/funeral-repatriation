The Falkland Islands are a British Overseas Territory in the South Atlantic, approximately 8,000 miles from the UK. English common law applies and all documentation is in English. The logistical challenge is purely one of distance and flight frequency.

Mount Pleasant Airport handles the RAF rotation and occasional LATAM commercial services via Chile. A specialist repatriation firm coordinates with the Falkland Islands government, the Coroner, and RAF logistics to plan the transfer.
