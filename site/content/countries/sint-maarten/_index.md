## Repatriation from Sint Maarten to the UK

Sint Maarten is a constituent country of the Kingdom of the Netherlands, occupying the southern portion of a small island in the northeastern Caribbean. The northern portion of the same island is **Saint Martin**, a French overseas collectivity — a separate jurisdiction with entirely different legal processes.

Philipsburg is Sint Maarten's capital. **Princess Juliana International Airport (SXM)**, famous for its runway approach over Maho Beach, is one of the Caribbean's most important aviation hubs.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The **British Embassy The Hague** (+31 70 427 0427) handles consular matters for Sint Maarten on a non-resident basis.

### The Legal Framework

Sint Maarten operates under Dutch civil law — autonomous within the Kingdom of the Netherlands. Death registration is handled by the **Civil Registry of Sint Maarten**. Non-natural deaths require investigation and clearance by the **Korps Politie Sint Maarten (KPSM)**.

### Island Division — Critical Note

The island is divided by a border with no formal customs checkpoint. The **southern half** is Sint Maarten (Dutch, this jurisdiction). The **northern half** is Saint Martin (French). If a death occurred on the French side, entirely different French legal processes apply. Confirm jurisdiction with local police or hospital before proceeding.

### Document Translation

Dutch documents require certified English translation for UK Coroner acceptance. Your specialist repatriation firm coordinates sworn translators.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate | Civil Registry of Sint Maarten |
| Certified English translation | Sworn translator |
| KPSM clearance | Korps Politie Sint Maarten (non-natural deaths) |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local funeral director |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Sint Maarten health authority |
| Export permit | Sint Maarten authorities |
| Consular registration | British Embassy, The Hague |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing to the UK

**Princess Juliana International Airport (SXM)** has direct **KLM** service to Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS), with frequent connections to UK airports. Also direct to Miami, New York, and other North American hubs as alternatives.

### Cremation

Cremation is available in Sint Maarten. Ashes may be returned to the UK in a sealed urn.
