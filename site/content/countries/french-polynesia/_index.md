## Repatriation from French Polynesia to the UK

French Polynesia is an overseas collectivity (collectivité d'outre-mer) of France, located in the central South Pacific Ocean approximately 15,700 km from the UK. It comprises five archipelagos: the Society Islands (including Tahiti and Bora Bora), the Tuamotu Archipelago, the Marquesas Islands, the Gambier Islands, and the Austral Islands. The capital is **Papeete** on Tahiti.

French Polynesia is one of the world's most sought-after long-haul destinations, attracting UK couples and families on honeymoon and major holidays.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The **British Embassy Paris** (+33 1 44 51 31 00) handles consular matters for French Polynesia on a non-resident basis.

### The Legal Framework

French Polynesia operates under **French civil law**. Death registration is handled by the **Service de l'État Civil** (civil registration service) — the relevant mairie or administrative office where the death occurred. Non-natural deaths require investigation by the **Gendarmerie nationale** (in rural areas and outer islands) or **Police nationale** (in Papeete).

All documents are issued in French. Certified English translation is required for UK Coroner acceptance.

### Outer Island Complexity

French Polynesia's outer islands present significant logistical challenges:

- **Bora Bora, Moorea**: Air Tahiti shuttle to Papeete (30–60 minutes). Reasonable infrastructure.
- **Marquesas Islands**: Air Tahiti connections to Papeete, 3–4 hours. Very limited local mortuary facilities.
- **Tuamotu Atolls**: Remote atolls with limited infrastructure. Inter-island transfers may be weather-dependent.
- **Austral Islands, Gambier Islands**: Extremely remote. Extended timelines should be expected.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Acte de décès (death certificate) | Service de l'État Civil / mairie |
| Certified English translation | Sworn translator |
| Gendarmerie/Police nationale clearance | (Non-natural deaths) |
| Certificat d'embaumement | Licensed local funeral director |
| Certificat de non-contagion | Health authority |
| Autorisation de sortie du territoire | French Polynesian authorities |
| Consular death registration | British Embassy, Paris |
| UK Coroner permission | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing to the UK

**Faa'a International Airport (PPT)** in Papeete has direct **Air France** and **Air Tahiti Nui** service to Paris Charles de Gaulle (CDG), approximately 22 hours. From Paris CDG, UK connections are frequent.

### Cremation

Cremation is available in Papeete. It is not available on outer islands. Ashes may be returned to the UK in a sealed urn.
