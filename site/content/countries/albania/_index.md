Albania has become an increasingly popular destination for British visitors seeking an affordable alternative to the Croatian or Greek coast. The Albanian Riviera and city of Gjirokaster are particular draw points. Deaths in Albania are manageable for UK repatriation specialists, but the process is less streamlined than EU member states.

## Legal framework

Albania is not an EU member but is a Council of Europe member and signatory to the Council of Europe Agreement on the Transfer of Corpses (Strasbourg, 1973 — ETS No. 080). Embalming and a laissez-passer are required for international transfer. Albanian domestic law also applies.

## Death registration

Deaths are registered at the local RGJC (Regjistri Gjendjes Civile — National Civil Registry) office. The Certifikate Vdekje (death certificate) is issued in Albanian. All documentation is in Albanian and requires certified translation. For unnatural deaths, the police are notified and the Prokuroria (Prosecutor's Office) takes over.

## British Embassy Tirana

The British Embassy in Tirana must be notified promptly. Address: Rruga Skenderbej 12, Tirana. Phone: +355 4 223 4973. FCDO 24-hour emergency line: +44 1908 516666.

## Prosecutor's post-mortem

When the Prosecutor orders a post-mortem, the Institut i Mjekesise Ligjore (Institute of Forensic Medicine) in Tirana conducts the examination. Forensic capacity outside the capital is limited; deaths in coastal areas or inland towns typically require transfer to Tirana for examination. Results take 7 to 14 days after the examination.

## Embalming and laissez-passer

Embalming is mandatory under the Strasbourg Agreement. The laissez-passer is issued by the Ministry of Interior or local prefecture. The process takes 3 to 7 days once all documents are in order.

## Flights from Tirana

Tirana International Nene Tereza Airport (TIA) has direct flights to London Luton and other UK airports with Ryanair and Wizz Air. Frequency is lower than major Western European hubs. Your specialist will plan cargo routing around available departures.
