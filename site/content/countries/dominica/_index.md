## Repatriation from Dominica to the UK

Dominica (not to be confused with the Dominican Republic) is a volcanic island in the Eastern Caribbean, known as the "Nature Isle" for its dramatic mountainous terrain, rainforest, and hot springs. It is a Commonwealth nation with English common law and a relatively straightforward civil registration system. British nationals visit primarily for ecotourism and diving.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The British High Commission Bridgetown, Barbados (+1 246 430 7800) handles consular matters for Dominica on a non-resident basis.

### The Legal Framework

Death registration is handled by the **Registrar General's Office**. The Registrar General issues the official death certificate in English. Where death is not from natural causes, the **Coroner's Court** must issue clearance before the body can be released. All documentation is in English throughout.

### Terrain and Ground Transport

Dominica's interior is one of the Caribbean's most rugged — a volcanic landscape with steep mountains, narrow roads, and dense rainforest. If a death occurs away from Roseau or the main coastal settlements, ground transport to Douglas-Charles Airport on the north-east coast can be significantly challenging. A specialist firm with local ground contacts is essential in these cases.

**Douglas-Charles Airport (DOM)** has a short runway, which limits aircraft types to smaller regional jets and turboprops. All international connections go via Antigua or Barbados.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate | Registrar General's Office |
| Coroner's certificate | Coroner's Court (non-natural deaths) |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local mortuary |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Ministry of Health |
| Export permit | Relevant authority |
| Consular death registration | BHC Bridgetown |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing and Transport

All international repatriation routes via **Antigua (ANU)** or **Barbados (BGI)**, connecting to UK airports. Caribbean Airlines and LIAT serve the regional routes.

### Cremation

Cremation is not available in Dominica. Full body repatriation to the UK is required in all cases.
