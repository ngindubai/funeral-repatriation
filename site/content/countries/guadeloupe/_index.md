## Repatriation from Guadeloupe to the UK

Guadeloupe is an archipelago in the Caribbean Sea and an overseas region and department (région et département d'outre-mer) of the French Republic — and an outermost region of the European Union. The main territory consists of two islands: **Basse-Terre** (the western volcanic island, administrative capital) and **Grande-Terre** (the eastern limestone island, economic centre and home to Pointe-à-Pitre). Guadeloupe is a well-known Caribbean destination for French and UK visitors.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The **British Embassy Paris** (+33 1 44 51 31 00) handles consular matters for Guadeloupe on a non-resident basis.

### The Legal Framework

As a French department, Guadeloupe uses the same civil law as metropolitan France. Death registration is through the **mairie** of the commune of death. Non-natural deaths require investigation by the **Gendarmerie nationale** or **Police nationale**. All documentation is in French.

### Jurisdiction Note: Saint-Barthélemy and Saint-Martin

**Saint-Barthélemy (St Barts)** and the **French side of Saint-Martin** are separate overseas collectivities of France — they are not administratively part of Guadeloupe despite their geographic proximity. If a death occurred on one of these islands, the process still follows French civil law but through different local administrations.

### Outer Islands

Deaths on **Marie-Galante**, **Les Saintes** (Îles des Saintes), or **La Désirade** require inter-island transfer by ferry or light aircraft to Grande-Terre for mortuary services and international departure.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Acte de décès (death certificate) | Mairie of commune of death |
| Certified English translation | Sworn translator |
| Gendarmerie/Police nationale clearance | (Non-natural deaths) |
| Certificat d'embaumement | Licensed local funeral director |
| Certificat de non-contagion | Health authority |
| Autorisation de sortie du territoire | French authorities |
| Consular death registration | British Embassy, Paris |
| UK Coroner permission | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing to the UK

**Pointe-à-Pitre Les Abymes International Airport (PTP)** on Grande-Terre has direct **Air France** service to Paris Charles de Gaulle (CDG), approximately 8.5 hours. UK connections from CDG are frequent.

### Cremation

Cremation is available in Guadeloupe. Ashes may be returned to the UK in a sealed urn.
