## Repatriation from Micronesia to the UK

The Federated States of Micronesia (FSM) is a sovereign Pacific island nation comprising four states — Yap, Chuuk, Pohnpei, and Kosrae — scattered across 2,600 km of the western Pacific Ocean. The total land area is just 702 km², but the ocean territory is enormous. The capital, Palikir, is on Pohnpei island.

FSM is associated with the United States in a Compact of Free Association. This shapes its documentation and civil registration standards. British nationals visit primarily for specialist diving, particularly in Chuuk Lagoon, one of the world's premier wreck diving destinations.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The British Embassy Manila (+63 2 8858 2200) handles consular matters for FSM on a non-resident basis.

### The Legal Framework

Each of the four FSM states operates its own vital statistics office and death registration system. Documentation is in English throughout. Non-natural deaths require police investigation and clearance before a body can be released for export.

### The Scale Problem

The single most important factor in any FSM repatriation is geography. A death on Yap requires completely different routing to a death in Chuuk Lagoon or on Kosrae — the two are over 2,000 km apart. Deaths on outer islands within any state require internal inter-island transfer (boat or light aircraft) to the state capital, often taking additional days.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate | Relevant state vital statistics office |
| Police clearance | FSM state police (non-natural deaths) |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local mortuary |
| Export permit | FSM national government |
| Consular death registration | British Embassy, Manila |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing and Transport

All international repatriation routes via **Guam (GUM)** on United Airlines, then connecting to Manila, Tokyo, or Honolulu for UK flights. The **Island Hopper** service links Guam–Chuuk–Pohnpei–Kosrae; Yap has separate Guam service via Pacific Island Air or charter.

No direct Pacific–UK services exist.

### Cremation

Cremation is not available in any FSM state. Full body repatriation to the UK is required.
