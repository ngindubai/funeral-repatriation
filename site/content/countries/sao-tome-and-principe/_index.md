## Repatriation from São Tomé and Príncipe to the UK

São Tomé and Príncipe is a small two-island nation in the Gulf of Guinea, approximately 300 kilometres west of Gabon. It is a Portuguese-speaking former colony with a civil registration system that follows Portuguese law. There is no resident UK diplomatic presence; the British High Commission in Yaoundé, Cameroon holds non-resident consular responsibility.

For urgent consular assistance, contact the FCDO on 020 7008 5000 (24 hours). The British High Commission Yaoundé can be reached on +237 222 220 796.

### The Legal Framework

Death registration in São Tomé and Príncipe is handled by the **Conservatória do Registo Civil** on whichever island the death occurred. An export permit must be obtained from the **Ministério do Interior** before remains can leave the country. The health ministry issues the freedom from infection certificate, which is a prerequisite for the export permit.

All documentation is in Portuguese. Certified English translations are required before UK authorities will accept any documents.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate (certidão de óbito) | Conservatória do Registo Civil |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local mortuary |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Ministry of Health |
| Export permit | Ministério do Interior |
| Consular death registration | British High Commission, Yaoundé |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

### Island Logistics

The country has two main islands: **São Tomé** (the main island and capital) and **Príncipe** (a smaller island 150 kilometres to the north-east). All international repatriation flights depart from **São Tomé International Airport (TMS)**.

If death occurs on Príncipe island, the remains must be transferred to São Tomé by internal flight (operated by STP Airways) or charter before international repatriation can begin. The internal flight schedule is limited, and this adds time to the overall process.

Cold storage and mortuary capacity on both islands is limited compared to larger African cities. A specialist repatriation firm should confirm storage arrangements before documentation begins.

### Routing and Transport

The primary repatriation routing options are:

- **Via Lisbon**: TAP Air Portugal operates regular São Tomé to Lisbon service, with straightforward UK connections from Lisbon.
- **Via Luanda**: TAAG Angola Airlines provides an alternative connection to Europe via Luanda.
