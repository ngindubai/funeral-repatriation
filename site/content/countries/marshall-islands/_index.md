## Repatriation from Marshall Islands to the UK

The Republic of Marshall Islands (RMI) is a sovereign nation in the central Pacific Ocean, comprising 29 atolls and 5 isolated islands with a total land area of just 181 km², spread across an Exclusive Economic Zone of approximately 1.9 million km². The capital is Majuro. RMI is associated with the United States in a Compact of Free Association.

The Marshall Islands are best known outside the Pacific for nuclear testing history (Bikini and Enewetak atolls), diving, and as the world's largest ship registry. British nationals visit in small numbers for specialist diving and business.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The British Embassy Manila (+63 2 8858 2200) handles consular matters for RMI on a non-resident basis.

### The Legal Framework

Death registration is handled by the **Vital Statistics Office** of the Republic of Marshall Islands. Non-natural deaths require **RMI Police Department** investigation and clearance. Documentation is in English throughout. US civil law and administrative influence remains significant from the trust territory period.

### The Outer Atoll Problem

The defining logistical challenge of any Marshall Islands repatriation is the possibility of death on an outer atoll. Majuro is the only practical international gateway. Travel from most inhabited outer atolls to Majuro requires boat or chartered light aircraft — journeys that can take anywhere from several hours to multiple days depending on weather and transport availability.

A specialist firm must establish the exact location of death before providing any time estimate.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate | Vital Statistics Office, RMI |
| Police clearance | RMI Police Department (non-natural deaths) |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local mortuary |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Ministry of Health |
| Export permit | Relevant authority |
| Consular death registration | British Embassy, Manila |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing and Transport

**Amata Kabua International Airport (MAJ)**, Majuro, is the main gateway. Air Marshall Islands provides inter-atoll services. International connections:

- **Honolulu (HNL)** — United Airlines, then US mainland connections to UK
- **Guam (GUM)** — United Airlines Island Hopper (infrequent)

No Pacific–UK direct services exist.

### Kwajalein

Kwajalein Atoll houses a major US Army installation (Ronald Reagan Ballistic Missile Defense Test Site). Deaths there involve US military coordination protocols, not standard RMI civil processes.

### Cremation

Cremation is not available in the Marshall Islands. Full body repatriation to the UK is required.
