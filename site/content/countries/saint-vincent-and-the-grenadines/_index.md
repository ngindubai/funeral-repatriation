## Repatriation from Saint Vincent and the Grenadines to the UK

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines is a Caribbean Commonwealth nation comprising the main island of Saint Vincent and a chain of smaller islands including Mustique, Bequia, Canouan, and Union Island to the south. English is the official language, common law governs civil registration, and the repatriation process is among the more straightforward for the Caribbean region.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The British High Commission Bridgetown, Barbados (+1 246 430 7800) handles consular matters for Saint Vincent and the Grenadines on a non-resident basis.

### The Legal Framework

Death registration in Saint Vincent and the Grenadines is handled by the **Registrar General's Office**. The Registrar General issues the official death certificate. Where death is not from natural causes, the **Coroner's Court** must issue a certificate before the body can be released. Documentation is in English throughout — no translation required.

### The Grenadines Island Consideration

If the death occurs on one of the outlying Grenadines islands — Mustique, Bequia, Canouan, or Union Island — an internal transfer to Saint Vincent's Argyle International Airport is required before international repatriation can begin. This transfer uses inter-island ferry services or light aircraft. A specialist firm will coordinate this as part of the overall repatriation.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate | Registrar General's Office |
| Coroner's certificate | Coroner's Court (non-natural deaths) |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local mortuary |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Ministry of Health |
| Export permit | Relevant authority |
| Consular death registration | BHC Bridgetown |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing and Transport

**Argyle International Airport (SVD)** is the main international gateway on Saint Vincent, handling Caribbean connections. Most UK-bound repatriation routing goes via **Barbados (BGI — Grantley Adams International)**, with onward connections to UK airports. Caribbean Airlines and LIAT serve the inter-island network.

### Cremation

Cremation is available in Saint Vincent. Repatriation of ashes to the UK is a practical alternative to full body repatriation.
