Seville Airport (SVQ) is the main airport for Andalucía's capital and one of southern Spain's principal international gateways. Seville sits in the interior, 90 kilometres from the Costa de la Luz and a similar distance from the Costa del Sol. British visitors and long-term residents in the region are among those whose deaths most frequently require repatriation from SVQ.

## Seville Airport and cargo routing

Seville Airport operates direct UK passenger services to London Heathrow (British Airways), Gatwick, and Manchester. For cargo repatriation, British Airways is the primary carrier from SVQ with direct LHR service. Alternatively, some cases route via Madrid Barajas (MAD, approximately 5 hours by road or 1 hour by air) for connections with more frequent cargo departures to the UK.

The licensed Seville funeral director advises on the direct SVQ versus MAD routing based on availability and the state of documentation at the time of booking.

## Spanish Juzgado de Instrucción process in Seville

Deaths in Seville that are unexpected, sudden, or occur without a clear physician-certified natural cause are referred to the Juzgado de Instrucción. The Juzgado orders a judicial autopsy (autopsia médico-forense) before releasing the body. This is the standard Spanish process and applies across Andalucía.

Once released by the Juzgado, the death is registered at the Registro Civil in Seville. Certified copies of the death certificate are available from the Registro Civil.

Processing times: Physician-certified natural deaths — 3 to 7 working days for registration and certificate. Juzgado cases: 2 to 4 weeks. Criminal investigation cases: allow 6 weeks or more.

Source: Ministerio de Justicia España, *Registro Civil*, 2024; Junta de Andalucía, *Servicios Funerarios*, 2024.

## Documentation for export from Seville

- Spanish death certificate (Registro Civil certified copy)
- Juzgado release document where applicable
- Licencia de enterramiento / export authorisation
- Embalming certificate
- Freedom from infection certificate
- Airline cargo booking confirmation (SVQ-LHR or via MAD)
- Passport of the deceased

## British consular contacts

The British Consulate in Seville (Plaza Nueva 8) handles consular registration and documentary assistance for British nationals in Andalucía. This is the correct first point of contact for Seville and the surrounding region. FCDO 24-hour emergency line: +44 (0)20 7008 5000.

## Typical timeline from Seville

Direct cargo route, no Juzgado involvement: 7 to 14 days. Juzgado case: 14 to 28 days. Criminal investigation: allow 6 weeks or more.

## Key local considerations

Seville experiences extreme summer heat — July and August regularly exceed 40°C. This has practical implications for repatriation logistics. Bodies deteriorate faster in high temperatures, which makes rapid embalming and refrigerated storage critical. The licensed local funeral home manages this, but families should be aware that timelines in midsummer cannot be extended without risk.

Many British nationals live in the Andalucía region long-term rather than as tourists. For residents, the Seville consulate advises that death registration may also involve notifying the FCDO death registration system. This does not change the practical process but should be understood.
