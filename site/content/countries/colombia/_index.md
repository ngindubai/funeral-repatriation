Colombia has undergone a significant transformation as a tourist destination in the past decade. British visitors are drawn to Cartagena's walled city, the coffee region around Salento, the Lost City trek, and Bogota's cultural scene. Repatriation from Colombia requires a specialist with Latin America experience; the documentation chain is unlike most European or Asian origins.

## Legal framework

Colombia is not a party to the Strasbourg Agreement. Repatriation is governed by Colombian domestic law and bilateral consular conventions between Colombia and the UK. The process involves multiple government agencies.

## Death registration

Deaths must be registered with a Notaria (notary office) to obtain the Registro Civil de Defuncion. This is distinct from the medical certificate of death issued by a hospital. The Registro Civil de Defuncion is the legally recognised document for international purposes. Source: Colombian Civil Registry Code (2023).

## British Embassy Bogota

The British Embassy in Bogota must be notified immediately. Address: Carrera 9 No. 76-49, Bogota. Phone: +57 1 326 8300. FCDO 24-hour emergency line: +44 1908 516666. The Embassy provides consular death registration and advises on current Colombian procedures.

## Instituto de Medicina Legal (IML)

For violent, sudden, or suspicious deaths, the body is referred to the Instituto de Medicina Legal y Ciencias Forenses (IML). The IML conducts forensic post-mortems. For criminal cases, the Fiscalia General de la Nacion (Attorney General's Office) is also involved and must issue an authorisation before release.

IML processing times vary: in Bogota and Medellin, results typically arrive within 7 to 14 days. In smaller cities (Cartagena, Cali, Barranquilla), allow longer.

## INVIMA export permit

The Instituto Nacional de Vigilancia de Medicamentos y Alimentos (INVIMA) issues the health permit required for exporting human remains. The INVIMA permit is specific to Colombia and often unknown to funeral directors without Latin America experience. Allow 3 to 7 days for the permit once all other documents are in place.

## Embalming and air cargo

Embalming is mandatory for international transfer. Colombia does not have zinc-lined coffin manufacturing in all regions; your specialist will confirm requirements with the local funeral director. Bogota's El Dorado Airport (BOG) handles the vast majority of international human remains cargo.

## Routing to the UK

There are no direct flights from Colombia to the UK. Standard routing: Bogota via Miami to London Heathrow, or via Panama City and on to Heathrow. Avianca and Copa Airlines are the primary carriers for the first leg. Allow for cargo booking delays on multi-leg routes.
