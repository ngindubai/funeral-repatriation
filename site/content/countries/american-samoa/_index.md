American Samoa is an unincorporated territory of the United States in the South Pacific. US federal law governs body repatriation — this is entirely separate from the standard FCDO-led process that applies in most countries. UK families face two parallel legal systems: US territorial requirements for export, and UK Coroner requirements for import.

The British High Commission in Wellington, New Zealand, handles formal consular matters. The Honorary British Consul in Apia, Samoa, is the nearest on-the-ground contact.
