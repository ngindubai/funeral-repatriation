## Repatriation from New Caledonia to the UK

New Caledonia (Nouvelle-Calédonie) is a special collectivity (collectivité sui generis) of France located in the South Pacific Ocean approximately 1,200 km east of Australia. The capital is **Nouméa**. It comprises the main island (Grande Terre), the Loyalty Islands, the Isle of Pines (Île des Pins), and several smaller island groups.

New Caledonia is known for its French culture, Kanak indigenous heritage, major nickel mining industry, and coral reef coastline. UK visitors include tourists and professionals working in the mining sector.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The **British Embassy Paris** (+33 1 44 51 31 00) handles consular matters for New Caledonia on a non-resident basis.

### The Legal Framework

New Caledonia has its own government (Gouvernement de la Nouvelle-Calédonie) and significant autonomy, but **French civil law** applies to death registration. The **Service de l'État Civil** handles registration. Non-natural deaths require investigation by the **Gendarmerie nationale** or **Police nationale**.

All documentation is in French.

### Civil Unrest Note

New Caledonia held three independence referendums in 2018, 2020, and 2021 — all returned votes to remain part of France, though by narrowing margins. Civil unrest has occurred, particularly in 2024. Check FCDO travel advice for the current situation before and during travel.

### Outer Islands

Deaths on the **Loyalty Islands** (Maré, Lifou, Ouvéa) or the **Isle of Pines** require inter-island Air Calédonie transfer to Nouméa before international repatriation can proceed.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Acte de décès (death certificate) | Service de l'État Civil |
| Certified English translation | Sworn translator |
| Gendarmerie/Police nationale clearance | (Non-natural deaths) |
| Certificat d'embaumement | Licensed local funeral director |
| Certificat de non-contagion | Health authority |
| Autorisation de sortie du territoire | New Caledonian/French authorities |
| Consular death registration | British Embassy, Paris |
| UK Coroner permission | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing to the UK

**La Tontouta International Airport (NOU)** near Nouméa has direct **Air France** and **Aircalin** (Air Calédonie International) service to Paris Charles de Gaulle (CDG), approximately 22 hours. Connections via **Sydney (SYD)** or **Auckland (AKL)** are alternatives for UK routing.

### Cremation

Cremation is available in Nouméa. Ashes may be returned to the UK in a sealed urn.
