Panama attracts a diverse mix of British visitors. Backpackers pass through Panama City en route between South America and Costa Rica. Retirees have settled here drawn by the Pensionado visa, which offers significant discounts across services. Canal tourists arrive on cruise ships. Adventure travellers head to Bocas del Toro on the Caribbean coast or the Chiriqui Highlands for hiking around Boquete.

Panama City is sophisticated by Central American standards, with good international hospitals and strong aviation links. Tocumen International Airport (PTY) is one of Latin America's major hubs, connecting to Miami, Madrid, Bogota, and beyond.

## The Consular Position

The British Embassy is resident in Panama City. For most cases, direct consular support is available without the multi-country accreditation arrangements common across the rest of Central America.

Your first call should be to the British Embassy Panama City and the FCDO Emergency Travel Line: 020 7008 5000.

## The Forensic Process

Natural deaths are registered via the Registro Civil. Unnatural deaths, including all accidents, drownings, and violent deaths, go to the Instituto de Medicina Legal y Ciencias Forenses (IMELCF). IMELCF has the main forensic pathology capacity in Panama. The process takes 4 to 6 weeks for unnatural deaths.

All documentation is in Spanish. Certified translation into English is required for UK death registration, probate, and insurance purposes.

## Bocas del Toro

The Bocas del Toro archipelago on the Caribbean coast is a popular backpacker zone with boat-based activity, surfing, and snorkelling. The islands have no bridge to the mainland. Transfer requires water taxi to Almirante, then road to David or Changuinola airports. This adds 2 to 4 days before the main repatriation process can begin. The Bocas del Toro Hospital has limited capacity.

## Chiriqui Highlands

The area around Boquete attracts eco-tourism, coffee-farm tours, and hiking. Deaths in this region require road transfer to David (approximately 40 minutes). Hospital Chiriqui in David handles regional cases.

## The Darien Province

The Darien Gap has no road connections. Any death here requires helicopter evacuation and involves Panamanian border security as well as standard repatriation authorities. These are rare but extremely complex cases.

## Cargo Routing

Tocumen International Airport (PTY) offers cargo routing via Miami (American Airlines, United), Madrid (Iberia), and Bogota (Copa Airlines, with connection to Europe). Options are better than most of Central America.
