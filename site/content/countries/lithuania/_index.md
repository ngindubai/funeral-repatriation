## Repatriation from Lithuania to the UK

Lithuania is a Northern European EU, NATO, and Schengen member state on the Baltic Sea, bordering Latvia, Belarus, Poland, and Kaliningrad (a Russian exclave). The capital is Vilnius. Lithuania joined the EU in 2004 and adopted the euro in 2015.

Lithuania has a large diaspora in the UK — one of the largest of any EU country. Many UK-Lithuania family connections exist, meaning repatriation cases in both directions are relatively common for specialist firms.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours) or the British Embassy Vilnius directly at +370 5 246 2900.

### The Legal Framework

Death registration in Lithuania is handled through the **Registrų centras** (Register Centre), specifically its Civil Metrical Records division. The official death certificate (Mirties liudijimas) is issued in Lithuanian. Non-natural deaths require investigation by the **Lithuanian Police** and, where necessary, clearance from the **Prokuratūra** (Prosecutor's Office) before the body can be released.

### Document Translation

The Lithuanian death certificate requires certified translation into English for the UK Coroner. Your specialist repatriation firm will arrange this.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate (Mirties liudijimas) | Registrų centras |
| Certified English translation | Sworn translator |
| Lithuanian Police / Prokuratūra clearance | Lithuanian authorities (non-natural deaths) |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed Lithuanian funeral director |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Health authority |
| Approved sealed coffin documentation | Funeral director |
| Consular death registration | British Embassy, Vilnius |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing to the UK

**Vilnius International Airport (VNO)** has direct flights to London Gatwick and Luton with Ryanair and Wizz Air. **Kaunas International Airport (KUN)** also serves London routes. Flight time is approximately 3 hours.

### Cremation

Cremation is available in Lithuania. Crematoria operate in Vilnius and Kaunas. Ashes are returned to the UK in a sealed urn.
