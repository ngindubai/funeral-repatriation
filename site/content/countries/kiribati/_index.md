## Repatriation from Kiribati to the UK

Kiribati (pronounced "Kiribas") is a sovereign Pacific nation comprising 33 atolls and reef islands spread across three island groups: the Gilbert Islands, the Phoenix Islands, and the Line Islands. The total land area is just 811 km², but the ocean territory spans approximately 3.5 million km² — straddling both the equator and the International Date Line. The capital, South Tarawa, is in the Gilbert Islands group.

Kiribati is one of the world's most climate-vulnerable nations: sea-level rise poses an existential threat to the low-lying atolls. The population is approximately 119,000, concentrated mainly in South Tarawa.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The British High Commission Suva, Fiji (+679 322 9100) handles consular matters for Kiribati on a non-resident basis.

### Three Groups — Three Very Different Cases

The most important factor in a Kiribati repatriation is which island group the death occurred in:

**Gilbert Islands** — including South Tarawa, where most of the population lives. Bonriki International Airport (TRW) has Fiji Airways service to Suva/Nadi. Most straightforward Kiribati cases come from here.

**Phoenix Islands** — one of the most remote inhabited island groups on Earth. Kanton Island is the only permanently inhabited island. Air links to South Tarawa are extremely infrequent. A death here may add weeks to the timeline.

**Line Islands** — including Kiritimati (Christmas Island), approximately 3,500 km east of South Tarawa. Some direct United Airlines service via Honolulu makes this group potentially faster to repatriate via the USA than via South Tarawa.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate | Kiribati Registration of Births, Deaths and Marriages |
| Police clearance | Kiribati Police Service (non-natural deaths) |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local mortuary |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Health authority |
| Export permit | Relevant authority |
| Consular death registration | BHC Suva, Fiji |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing to the UK

**Gilbert Islands:** Via Fiji (NAN/SUV) then Sydney, Singapore, or Hong Kong to UK.
**Line Islands (Kiritimati):** May route via Honolulu (HNL) then to UK.
**Phoenix Islands:** Via South Tarawa first, then as per Gilbert Islands routing.

### Cremation

Cremation is not available in Kiribati. Full body repatriation is required.
