## Repatriation from Nicaragua to the UK

Nicaragua is a Central American nation governed by President Daniel Ortega, who has held power since 2007. Following a violent crackdown on protests in 2018, the government has maintained an authoritarian grip on civil society. FCDO advises against non-essential travel. For urgent consular assistance, contact the FCDO on 020 7008 5000 (24 hours). The British Embassy San José can be reached on +506 2258 2025.

### Political Context for Repatriation

Nicaragua's government maintains strict controls over the movement and activities of foreign nationals. Interactions with Nicaraguan authorities can be unpredictable in ways that affect the documentation process. A specialist repatriation firm with current in-country contacts is the most reliable way to navigate this environment.

In Managua, the civil registration system functions and the standard documentation pathway — Registro Civil, Ministerio del Interior export permit — is well-established. The risk of delays is higher for cases involving foreign nationals perceived to have any connection to political activity.

### The Legal Framework

Death registration in Nicaragua is handled by the **Registro del Estado Civil de las Personas (RECP)**, administered under the Consejo Supremo Electoral. The **Ministerio del Interior** issues the export permit once death certification and health documentation are in order.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Certificado de defunción (death certificate) | Registro del Estado Civil de las Personas |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local mortuary |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Ministry of Health |
| Export permit | Ministerio del Interior |
| Consular death registration | British Embassy, San José |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

All Spanish-language documents require certified English translation before UK authorities will process them.

### Routing and Transport

Managua's Augusto C. Sandino International Airport (MGA) is the primary international gateway. Standard routing options:

- **Via Miami**: American Airlines operates Managua to Miami, with direct UK connections.
- **Via Panama City**: Copa Airlines connects Managua to Panama City Tocumen, with onward European connections.
- **Via San José, Costa Rica**: Regional connections via San José provide an alternative routing.

### Remote Areas

The Caribbean Coast Autonomous Regions (RACN and RACCS) in eastern Nicaragua are remote, with limited road infrastructure. Deaths in jungle or coastal communities in these regions require specialist ground logistics before official documentation can begin. The Río Coco border region with Honduras also has elevated security risk.

### Cremation

Cremation services are limited in Nicaragua. Repatriation of the body to the UK is the standard and most straightforward approach for UK nationals.
