Costa Rica is Central America's most established tourist destination for British visitors. The national park system, wildlife, and adventure sports draw eco-tourists. The Nicoya Peninsula and Caribbean coast attract beach visitors. Most deaths involving British nationals occur in the Central Valley (San Jose area) or popular tourist regions.

## Legal framework

Costa Rica is not a signatory to the Strasbourg Agreement. Repatriation is governed by Costa Rican domestic law. The OIJ (Organismo de Investigacion Judicial — Judicial Investigation Police) and the Ministry of Health are the primary authorities.

## Death registration

Deaths are registered at the Registro Civil (RCEC — Registro Civil del Estado Civil) in the canton where the death occurred. The Certificado de Defuncion is the official death certificate. For hospital deaths in San Jose, registration is straightforward. For unnatural deaths anywhere in the country, the OIJ takes jurisdiction immediately.

## British Embassy San Jose

The British Embassy in San Jose must be notified on the day of death. Address: Edificio Centro Colon, Paseo Colon, San Jose. Phone: +506 2258 2025. FCDO 24-hour emergency line: +44 1908 516666.

## OIJ investigation and post-mortem

The OIJ handles all criminal, violent, and suspicious death investigations. For deaths in national parks or involving apparent accidents in the field, the OIJ deploys to the scene. The body cannot be moved until OIJ has cleared it. Post-mortems are conducted at INCIENSA (National Institute for Epidemiological Research) or OIJ forensic facilities in San Jose. Results typically available within 7 to 14 days.

## Ministry of Health export permit

The Ministerio de Salud issues the export permit for human remains. Required documents: Certificado de Defuncion, OIJ release, embalming certificate, freedom from contagious disease clearance. Allow 3 to 7 days for the permit.

## Routing to the UK

No direct flights operate from Costa Rica to the UK. Standard routing: San Jose Juan Santamaria (SJO) via Miami to London Heathrow with American Airlines or United. Copa Airlines via Panama City is an alternative. For deaths in the Guanacaste region, cargo may route via Liberia Airport (LIR) or via San Jose.
