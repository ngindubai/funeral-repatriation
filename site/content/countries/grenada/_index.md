## Repatriation from Grenada: What Families Need to Know

Grenada is known as the Spice Isle and attracts British tourists for its beaches, diving, and sailing. It also hosts St George's University, one of the Caribbean's largest medical schools, with a substantial student body that includes British nationals.

There is no resident British diplomatic post in Grenada. An Honorary British Consul operates in St George's. The British High Commission in Bridgetown, Barbados, has formal accreditation.

### Who to Call

1. Honorary British Consul St George's — first consular contact.
2. FCDO Emergency Travel Line: 020 7008 5000 (24 hours).
3. A specialist UK repatriation company.

### St George's University Deaths

St George's University School of Medicine has approximately 5,000 students on the Grenada campus at any one time, drawn from the UK, USA, Canada, and other countries. Deaths of British university students involve the university's student welfare and emergency response systems alongside the Honorary Consul. The university has established protocols with local authorities and the High Commission for these cases. This does not simplify the legal process, but it does mean local coordination is more practised than on many comparable islands.

### The Three Islands

The state of Grenada comprises three islands. Grenada (the main island) holds St George's, the hospital, and the airport. Carriacou is a smaller island 30 km to the north-east, reached by ferry from St George's (90 minutes) or small aircraft (15 minutes). Deaths on Carriacou require transfer to Grenada for full forensic examination.

Petite Martinique is a tiny island east of Carriacou with no hospital or clinic. Any serious medical event there requires immediate emergency evacuation to Carriacou or Grenada.

### Bianca C and Diving Deaths

The wreck of the Bianca C, a 182-metre Italian ocean liner sunk in 1961, lies off St George's harbour and is considered one of the Caribbean's best wreck dives. Diving-related deaths at Grenada require specialist forensic examination.

### Cargo Routing

Cargo connects via Maurice Bishop International Airport (GND) to Barbados (BGI) and then to London Gatwick via British Airways or Virgin Atlantic. Some cargo routes use Antigua or Trinidad as transit points.
