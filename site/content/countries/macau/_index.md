## Repatriation from Macau to the UK

Macau (officially Macao SAR) is a Special Administrative Region of China located on the western Pearl River Delta, approximately 60 km from Hong Kong. It was a Portuguese territory until 1999. Today it is one of the world's most densely populated places and a major destination for gaming tourism.

Under the "one country, two systems" principle, Macau operates its own legal, financial, and civil registration system, distinct from both mainland China and Hong Kong.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The **British Consulate General, Hong Kong** (+852 2901 3000) handles consular matters for Macau on a non-resident basis.

### The Legal Framework

Death registration in Macau is managed by the **Conservatória do Registo Civil** (Civil Registry Conservatory). Macau's legal system is based on Portuguese civil law — different from Hong Kong's common law and from mainland China's system.

Non-natural deaths require investigation and clearance by the **Polícia Judiciária** (Judicial Police), which is modelled on the Portuguese judicial police.

### Document Translation

Death certificates and official documents are issued in Chinese and/or Portuguese. Both require certified English translation for UK Coroner acceptance. Your specialist repatriation firm coordinates sworn translators.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate | Conservatória do Registo Civil |
| Certified English translation | Sworn translator (Chinese/Portuguese to English) |
| Polícia Judiciária clearance | Polícia Judiciária (non-natural deaths) |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local funeral director |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Health Bureau, Macau SAR |
| Export permit | Macau SAR Government |
| Consular registration | British CG, Hong Kong |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |

### Routing to the UK

**Macau International Airport (MFM)** serves regional destinations. In practice, most repatriations to the UK route via **Hong Kong International Airport (HKG)**, which has frequent direct flights to UK airports. The Macau–Hong Kong ferry crossing takes approximately 1 hour.

### Cremation

Cremation is available in Macau. The Macau SAR Government operates cremation facilities. Ashes may be returned to the UK in a sealed urn.
