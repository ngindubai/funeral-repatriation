## Repatriation from Andorra to the UK

Andorra is a small co-principality nestled in the Pyrenees between France and Spain. It has no airport of its own, no armed forces, and a population of around 80,000. Despite its size, it has a distinct civil and legal system with Catalan as the official language and French and Spanish legal influences. A number of British nationals visit each year for skiing, hiking, and duty-free shopping.

For consular assistance, contact the FCDO on **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). The British Embassy Madrid (+34 91 714 6300) handles consular matters for Andorra on a non-resident basis.

### The Legal Framework

Civil registration in Andorra is organised through the seven **Comuns** (parish authorities). Each Comú maintains civil records for its territory. The death is registered at the Comú for the parish where death occurred. Documentation is issued in Catalan, requiring certified English translation.

Where death is not from natural causes, the **Cos de Policia d'Andorra** investigates and must authorise release of the body.

### Routing: Barcelona or Toulouse

Andorra has no commercial airport. Repatriation routing uses one of two options:

- **Barcelona El Prat (BCN)**: Approximately 3 hours by road. Multiple direct flights to UK airports daily. Standard choice for most repatriations.
- **Toulouse-Blagnac (TLS)**: Approximately 3 hours by road via the northern route. Fewer direct UK connections but viable.

A specialist repatriation firm will arrange the hearse transfer and coordinate with Spanish or French authorities as needed.

### Cremation Options

There are no cremation facilities in Andorra. Where cremation is required, the body can be transferred to Spain (Barcelona) or France (Toulouse or Perpignan) for cremation, after which the ashes are repatriated to the UK. This adds a step to the process but is well-established and manageable.

### Required Documents

| Document | Issued By |
|---|---|
| Death certificate (in Catalan) | Parish Comú |
| Police clearance (non-natural deaths) | Cos de Policia d'Andorra |
| Embalming certificate | Licensed local mortuary |
| Freedom from infection certificate | Andorran Ministry of Health |
| Export authorisation | Relevant authority |
| Consular death registration | British Embassy, Madrid |
| UK Coroner permission (if applicable) | HM Coroner, UK |
