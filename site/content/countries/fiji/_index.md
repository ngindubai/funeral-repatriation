## Repatriation from Fiji: What Families Need to Know

Fiji is one of the more straightforward Pacific repatriations. Not easy — no international repatriation is easy — but less complicated than Vanuatu, Papua New Guinea, or the more remote island groups. The British High Commission Suva is experienced with British national deaths, English documentation reduces translation delays, and Fiji Airways provides reasonable connections through Sydney, Melbourne, and Auckland.

The key variable is where in Fiji the death occurred.

### Viti Levu versus the Outer Islands

Viti Levu is the main island and contains both Nadi International Airport and the capital Suva. Deaths in Suva, Nadi, the Coral Coast, or anywhere on Viti Levu proceed through the standard documentation chain without inter-island transfer delays.

Deaths on Vanua Levu (Savusavu, Labasa) require transfer to Viti Levu. Domestic flights connect Vanua Levu to Nadi and Suva, and this transfer typically takes one to two days.

Deaths in the Yasawa Islands, Mamanuca Islands, or the more remote Lau Group present the greatest logistical challenge. The Lau Group, in particular, is a scattered archipelago of atolls in eastern Fiji with infrequent inter-island boat services. A death on one of these outer islands can require two to three days just to get the body to Viti Levu.

### Forensic Processing

Post-mortems for unnatural deaths are conducted at Colonial War Memorial Hospital (CWMH) in Suva. Lautoka Hospital serves western Viti Levu. Vanua Levu and outer island deaths are transferred to CWMH Suva.

The documentation chain:

1. Death certificate from the Registrar of Births, Deaths and Marriages, Fiji.
2. Police investigation closure for unnatural deaths.
3. Post-mortem report from CWMH Suva or Lautoka Hospital.
4. Ministry of Health export permit.
5. Embalming to IATA standards, with certificate.

### Scuba Diving Deaths

Fiji is a world-class scuba diving destination, and diving deaths are among the most common causes of British tourist fatalities. Decompression illness deaths can require more detailed investigation — review of dive logs, equipment condition, and surface interval records. This typically adds 5 to 10 days to the overall timeline. Notify the British High Commission Suva and your repatriation provider immediately if the death involves diving.

### Cargo Routing

Fiji Airways connects Nadi International Airport (NAN) with Sydney, Melbourne, and Auckland. All repatriation cargo connects via one of these hubs before continuing to London. Qantas and Air New Zealand are also options from their respective hubs.
