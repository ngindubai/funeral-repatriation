The US Virgin Islands are a US organised territory in the Caribbean, purchased from Denmark in 1917. US federal law governs body export. The USVI Medical Examiner, based in St Croix, covers the entire territory.

The British Embassy in Washington DC handles formal consular matters. The Honorary British Consul in Barbados may also provide assistance for Eastern Caribbean cases. Routing via Miami or San Juan Puerto Rico makes connections to UK airports practical.
