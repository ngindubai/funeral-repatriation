## What to Do When Someone Dies in Micronesia

The Federated States of Micronesia is not a single island — it is four states (Yap, Chuuk, Pohnpei, Kosrae) spread across 2,600 km of Pacific Ocean. The first question your repatriation specialist will ask is: which state and which island?

### Step 1 — Contact the FCDO Immediately

Call **020 7008 5000** (24 hours). FCDO will contact the British Embassy Manila, which holds non-resident responsibility for FSM. British Embassy Manila: 120 Upper McKinley Road, McKinley Hill, Taguig City, Metro Manila (+63 2 8858 2200).

### Step 2 — Establish the Exact Location

Before any other step, establish:
- Which FSM state (Yap, Chuuk, Pohnpei, or Kosrae)
- Which island within that state
- Whether the body is accessible by scheduled air service or requires boat/light aircraft

The answer determines the entire timeline.

### Step 3 — Appoint a UK Repatriation Specialist

FSM is one of the most complex Pacific repatriation cases. You need a firm with specific experience in very remote Pacific islands and existing ground contacts in at least two of the four states.

### Step 4 — State-Level Documentation

Each FSM state has its own vital statistics office. The documentation process differs slightly between states, though all are in English. Your firm's local contacts will manage this.

### Step 5 — Routing

All international routing goes via **Guam (GUM)** on the United Airlines Island Hopper service, then connecting to Manila, Tokyo, or Honolulu for UK flights. Flight frequency is limited — typically three times per week on the Island Hopper.

### Realistic Timeline

Expect **21–56 days**. Cases in Pohnpei or Kosrae near the main airport tend towards the lower end. Cases in Chuuk or outer island locations tend towards the higher end.
